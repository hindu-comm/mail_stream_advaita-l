Dear members
Manyu has a meaning anger.I have not come across any distinct suktham 
devoted to manyu like Srisuktham,Durga suktham,Nakshatra suktham,Narayana 
suktham,Pavamana Suktham,Bhu Suktham,Purusha Suktham,Vishnu Suktham,Neela 
Suktham,Baghya Suktham and Saraswathi Suktham to name a few.May be Manyu 
Suktham refers to Sri Rudram as in it we pray to Lord Shiva to leave His 
anger on us and bless us.R.Krishnamoorthy.
