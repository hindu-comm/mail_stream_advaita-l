*Manyu sukta* is hymn <http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hymn> 10.83 and 10.84
from the Rig veda <http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Rig_veda>. It contains 14
verses and is dedicated to Manyu <http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Manyu>. Manyu
in Vedic sanskrit <http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Vedic_sanskrit> stands for
temper, anger or passion. The original deity that this hymn addresses is
believed to be Lord Narasimha <http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Narasimha>.
