Whether it is Samkhyakarika of Isvarakrishna or Tattvasamasa of Kapila or Sankhyasutra of Kapila or the Sankhya portionin the Bhagavd Gita or the Sankhya in the Mahabharata or Sankhya in Bhagavata purana I do not see any real difference among them. There are only some minor terminological differences. For example the word Tanmatra is not used by all the texts.
