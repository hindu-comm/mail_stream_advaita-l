On Wed, 9 Dec 2009, Shakthi Prashanth wrote:

> I asked this question bcoz, If the world is not apart from me, then 
> Brahman is complete only when everything in this universe knowns what & 
> who is Brahman. That is the actuall One-ness, right?

The world _is_ one and complete.  It is your ahamkara (ego) which 
interprets the data of your senses as evidence of multiplicity.  When that 
ahamkara is surpressed the evidence of incompleteness is also surpressed.
