In the pAtanjala yoga system, strictly speaking, only 1 below is described.
Every purusha is a drashTA and dRSi-mAtra, i.e. sAkshI and ISvara is a special
purusha. Neither in the yogasUtra nor in the bhAshya is there a discussion of
2 and 3 with respect to ISvara.
