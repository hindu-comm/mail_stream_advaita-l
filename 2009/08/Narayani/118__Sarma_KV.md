Sir,

At one level of awareness, not only
                                         Narayani = Durga = Lakshmi
but also,
                                         Narayani = Govinda = Siva.

At other levels below the above, not only
                                         Narayana =/= Durga =/= Laksmi
even
                                         Rama =/= Krishna!!

The truth, however, is: She is Durga, Lakshmi, ParaSakti, Paramaatma.. the
Self, everything.

"sarvam khalvidamEvaaham, naanyadasti sanaatanam" (EVERYTHING is I [=Devi];
there is nothing else primordial.) - from Devi Bhagavatam.

sOham
-Sarma KV
