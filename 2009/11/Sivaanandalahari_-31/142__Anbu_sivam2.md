Sivaanandalahari -31नालं वा परमोपकारक-मिदं त्वेकं पशूनां पते
पश्यन् कुक्षिगतान् चराचरगणान् बाह्यस्थितान् रक्षितुम् ।
सर्वामर्त्य-फलायनौषध-मतिज्वालाकरं भीकरं
निक्षिप्तं गरलं गले न गिलितं नोद्गीर्ण-मेव त्वया ॥ ३१ ॥

nAlaM vA paramopakAraka-midaM tvekaM paSUnAM pate
paSyan kukShigatAn charAcharagaNAn bAhyasthitAn rakShitum |
sarvAmartya-PalAyanauShadha-matijvAlAkaraM BIkaraM
nikShiptaM garalaM gale na gilitaM nodgIrNa-meva tvayA || 31 ||

Oh! Lord of all beings! Is the one single great help be not sufficient ? In
order to protect the moving and non moving living beings in your stomach and
others outside you neither swallowed nor spat but stopped at your throat the
deadly fearful fire producing herbal poison which makes the Devas flee.

*Commentary*

Aum

Hey! Parameswara! To know your great kaarunyam, endless sakthi and eternal
love, is not recalling your fantastic feat of aalaahala paanam enough! Hey!
Jagadeesaa! You have taken a vow to protect the entire universe and all the
living beings, is it not? The wonder of it all is that you put into your
mouth that terrible poison and when Paarvathi stopped you from swallowing
because she feared that the jeevaraasis and the worlds inside you would die,
yet you would not spit it out because you have taken the poison in to
protect the worlds outside! So you kept it in your throat and turned it into
an aabharaNam. No other god could have done this extraordinary feat! Thus
the very name ‘ThiruneelakaNtam’ stuns the bhaktha and turns him into
ecstacy!
