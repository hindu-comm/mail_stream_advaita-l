shrii miinaaxii stuti

of

shrii jagatguru shR^i~Ngagiri chandrasekhara bhaarati swamigaL

adraaxam bahubhaagyato guruvaraiH saMpuujyamaanaaM mudaa
pullanmallimukhaprasuunanivahairhaalaasyanaathapriyaam.h |
viiNaveNumR^ida~NgavaadyamuditaameNaa~Nga bimbaananaaM
kaaNaadaadisamastashaastramatitaam shoNaadharaaM shyaamaLaam.h || 1 ||

In a split form:

adraaxam bahu bhaagyato guruvaraiH saMpuujyamaanaaM mudaa
pullan.h  malli mukha prasuuna nivahair haalaasya naatha priyaam.h |
viiNa veNu mR^ida~Nga vaadyaM uditaaM eNaa~Nga bimbaananaaM
kaaNaadaadi samasta shaastra matitaam shoNaadharaaM shyaamaLaam.h || 1 ||

I saw shyaamaLa (miinaaxii) due to my good fortune who is worshipped
by the achaarya-s with joy using jasmine flowers, who is beloved of
Lord of haalasyapuri (sundareshvara), who rejoices listening to the
beautiful music of viiNa, flute and mR^ida~Nga, whose face is
beautiful like a moon, who bestows the knowledge of shastra-s composed
by sages like kaaNada, and whose lips are red in color.


Corrections are welcome.

Ravi
