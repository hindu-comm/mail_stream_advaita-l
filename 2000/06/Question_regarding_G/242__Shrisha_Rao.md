On Wed, 28 Jun 2000, Vidyasankar Sundaresan wrote:

> Shrisha, I think you sent me the vivekacUDAmaNi reference by private email,
> but I can't find it with me now. Could you point to the place where this
> reference is found? Modern academic scholars aside, I would like to check
> what Swami Chandaresekhara Bharati's commentary on vivekacUDAmaNi says in
> this regard.

 `mAyAmAtramidaM dvaitamadvaitaM paramArthataH' (GK I-17) |
  iti brUte shrutiH sAxAt.h sushhuptAvanubhUyate  ||

-- tagged as number 405 or 406 depending on the edition.

Sayana cites the same hemistich also in commenting upon the Tai. A. (don't
have the exact reference, but Bhattacharya's book should have it):

 `mAyAmAtramidaM dvaitamadvaitaM paramArthataH'  |
  iti mANDUkyashAkhAyAM shrutivAkyamudAhR^itam.h  ||

Regards,
