>The importance of the Gita in my opinion is that Arjunas predicament
>brings so many important issues together at once.
>
>1.  What is ones responsibility to ones self? To ones family?  To society?
>
>2.  What is the role of karma versus jnana?
>
>3.  When is action called for and when renunciation of action?
>
>4.  What is the nature of the Supreme?  How does one properly worship Him?
>
>All these are important Vedantic topics so its no wonder that thinkers of
>so many different views were attracted to it.
