On Sun, 11 Jun 2000, T Swaminarayan wrote:

> All the great Teachers and Acharyas said and realised the very same
> thing that Ramana said to his deciples! Why go any further? Do not
> confuse yourself by reading yet more texts and yet more explanations
> about your SELF.You have already once felt HIS presence in You.
>

Actually I think it is important for those who aren't familiar with the
tradition (not just Westerners but Indians too) to hear as many different
viewpoints as possible.  This is actually _less_ confusing in the long
run.  Especially when we are talking about meditation, yoga etc.these
things can be spiritually and physically dangerous if done incorrectly.
