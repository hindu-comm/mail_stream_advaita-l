namo'stute vyasavishAlabuddhe phullarAravindAyatapatranetre |
yena tvayA bhAratatailapUrNah prajvAlito jnAnamayah pradIpah ||

I bow down to you Oh Vyasa of great wisdom, whose eyes are like lotuses in
bloom, who has lit the lamp of Jnana with the oil of the Mahabharata.
