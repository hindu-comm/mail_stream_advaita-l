First the good news. Prof. Murthy told me that he will restart
his series on saundaryalaharii soon.

In the case of shivaanandalaharii we are fortunate to have
bhagavan ramaNa's selection of 10 verses which represent the gist
of the work. I do not know whether such a selection of verses
exists for saundaryalaharii.

In order to learn the text, I thought I will do in parts so that
it is easy to memorize and understand it in depth. Browsing
through the text (100 verses), I have selected a set of 10 verses
and then a set of 25 verses which I thought gives some of the
important verses in the text.  The 25-verses set has the 10-set
verses set  as its subset. I will give this list later.

Now coming to the 10-verses set. The verses are
1,3,8,9,11,27,32,33,98,100. The selection is based on what
appealed me the most.  I am attaching a text document which gives
these 10 verses. At this point I would solicit your
selection.  Please let us know the 10 verses you would select
from saundaryalaharii.


Thank you.

Ravi
