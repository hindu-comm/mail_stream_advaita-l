namaH shivaabhyaaM jagadiishvaraabhyaaM
 jagatpatibhyaaM jayavigrahaabhyaam.h |
jambhaari-mukhyair-abhi-vanditaabhyaaM
 namo namaH sha~Nkara-paarvatiibhyaam.h || 4||

Prostrations to the divine couple shrii shivaa and Lord shiva,
who are the controllers and Lord of the Universe, who are ever
victorious, who are worshipped by Lord indra and others. I bow
again and again to paarvati-devii and Lord shiva.

Corrections are welcome.

--
bhava shankara deshikame sharaNam
