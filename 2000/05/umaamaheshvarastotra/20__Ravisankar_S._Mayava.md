namaH shivaabhyaaM vR^ishhavaahanaabhyaaM
  viri~nchi-vishhNu-indra supuujitaabhyaam.h |
vibhuuti-paaTiira vilepanaabhyaam.h
 namo namaH sha~Nkara-paarvatiibhyaam.h  || 3 ||

Prostrations to the divine couple shrii shivaa and Lord shiva,
who has a bull as their vehicle, who is worshipped by Lord
brahma, Lord VishNu and Indra, and whose body is smeared with
sandalwood paste* and vibhuuti (ash). I bow again and again to
paarvati-devii and Lord shiiva.

Ravi

*ambaaL is fond of sandalwood paste. shrii lalitaa sahasranaamam
celebrates HER as chandana drava digdhaangi and shrii miinaakshii
asshTottaram praises HER as divya chandana digdhaangii. A brief
meaning to shrii miinaakshii ashhTottara is being posted at
http://www.egroups.com/group/ambaa-L and it is available in
collected form at  http://www.geocities.com/kaamaakshi/
