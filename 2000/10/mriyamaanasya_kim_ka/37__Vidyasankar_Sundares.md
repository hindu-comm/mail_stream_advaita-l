>narration, i feel. i am sorry once again. but this welling of an urgent
>desire for saadhana and abandon everything except my obligatory duties
>has forced me to write this message to you all.

Please don't worry about it. Focussing one's attention on what
matters is good. muNDaka reminds us "anyA vAco vimuncatha". Too
much talk is not good either, and when the desire to talk falls
away, there is no need to feel sorry about it.

Best wishes,
Vidyasankar
