  Meditation on Lord's Six Faces

sadaa shaaradaaH shhaNmR^igaaN^kaa yadi syuH
 samudyanta eva sthitaashchetsamantaat.h |
sadaa puurNabimbaaH kalaN^kaishcha hiinaa\-
 stadaa tvanmukhaanaaM bruve skanda saamyam.h || 13||

What can I compare to Thy six faces!  If there be six full moons free
from blemishes and if those moons be ever shining on all sides, even
then they would be no match to your effulgent faces.

  Meditation on Lord's Facial Features

sphuranmandahaasaiH saha.nsaani chaJNcha\-
 tkaTaakshaavaliibhR^iN^gasa.nghojjvalaani |
sudhaasyandibimbaadhaaraNiishasuuno
 tavaalokaye shhaNmukhaambhoruhaaNi || 14||


Oh! Son of Parameshwara, I see Thy six lotus smiling faces
shining like a group of swans, having gleaming side glance ever moving
like a row of bees and with red lips overflowing with nectar.

  Meditation on Lord's Twelve Eyes

vishaaleshhu karNaantadiirgheshhvajasraM
 dayaasyandishhu dvaadashasviikshaNeshhu |
mayiishhatkaTaakshaH sakR^itpaatitashche\-
 dbhavette dayaashiila kaa naam haaniH || 15||


Oh! Son of Parameshwara! Oh! Merciful Lord!  When Thou hast twelve
broad long eyes extending upto ears, what will Thou loose by casting
that side glance for a moment [on me]?

--
bhava shankara deshikame sharaNam
