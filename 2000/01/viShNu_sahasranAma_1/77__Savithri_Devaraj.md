12. muktAnAm paramA gatiH : The highest goal of the liberated ones.  For
one who attains to Him, there is neither rebirth nor attaining to any
thing higher, there being nothing higher than Him.

13. avyayaH : One for whom there is no decay.  He is described in sruti
as ' ajaro.amaro.avyayaH' - unaging, undying and undecaying.

----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

>From Translation and commentary of Sri SankarAcArya. Translated by
Swami Tapasyananda.

Savithri
