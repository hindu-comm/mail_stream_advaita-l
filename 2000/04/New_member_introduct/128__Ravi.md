> hills near Bombay).
>         Last year I got involved with an organization called The Philosophy
> Foundation (http://www.philosophyfoundation.org/). This is an American
> organization which draw heavily from the teachings of Sankaracharya. Here I
> had the opportunity to learn from people who had spent decades studying
> Advaita philosophy.
>         The foundation also maintains a great book store and I had the opportunity
> to read The Bhagavad Gita with the commentary of Sri Sankaracharya (Samata
> Books), Vasistha's Yoga (SUNY Press) etc.
>
