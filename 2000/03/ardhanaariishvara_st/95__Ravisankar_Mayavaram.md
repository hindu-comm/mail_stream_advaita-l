ambhodhara shyAmaLa kundalaayai
 taTitprabhaata jaTaadharaayai |
niriishvaraayai nikhileshvaraaya
 namaH shivaayai cha namaH shivaaya || 6 ||

SHE shines like a night lily with tresses like dark clouds and His raised
jaTa shines forth like dawn. SHE has none superior to HER and HE is the
Lord of all. Such is the wonderful form of ardhanaarishvara. I bow to
shrii shivaa and Lord shiva.
