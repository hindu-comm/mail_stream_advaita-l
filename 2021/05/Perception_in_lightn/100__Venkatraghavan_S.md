The upapatti is that science has demonstrated that the svabhAva of light is
to travel at 300,000 km/s and sound is to travel at 340 m/s.

That being so, one could postulate that the svabhAva of mind + shrotram is
to travel at 340 m/s vs 300,000 km/s for the mind + chakshu.
