The Sannyasopanishad, listed in the Muktika 108, prescribes a practice that
is in vogue among Advaitin Sannyasins.

No shikha, yajnopavita, one staff and bhasmadhaarana:

परमहंसः शिखायज्ञोपवीतरहित: पञ्चगृहेषु करपात्री एककौपीनधारी। शाटीमेकामेकं
वैणवं* दण्डमेकशाटीधरो वा भस्मोद्धूलनपर: सर्वत्यागी।॥*

This is also found in the Narada parivrajaka upanishad:

https://www.tititudorancea.com/z/narada_parivrajaka_upanishad_sanskrit_devanagari_with_transliteration.htm

https://sa.wikisource.org/s/6t5

https://sanskritdocuments.org/doc_upanishhat/muktika.html

तुरीयातीत*संन्यास*परिव्राजाक्षमालिका ।  अव्यक्तैकाक्षरं पूर्णा
सूर्याक्ष्यध्यात्मकुण्डिका ॥ ३६॥
