This clip talks about the life of Rudra Pashupathi Nayanar on how he was
eternally chanting RUDRAM. RUDRAM is the foremost hymn in the Vedas for
Lord Shiva which talks about his divine qualities and characteristics.
Rudra Pashupathi Nayanar was chanting RUDRAM eternally and also he had the
realization of Lord Shiva in every circumstance of his life and finally
attained Moksha.
