25th verse from sharabhopanishad

चतुर्भिश्च चतुर्भिश्च द्वाभ्यां पञ्चमिरेव च । हूयते च पुनर्द्वाभ्यां स मे
विष्णुः प्रसीदतु
By 4 + 4 + 2 + 5 +2 = 17 letters Vishnu is offered oblations in fire
sacrifices
May that Vishnu (Parameshwara) who is worshipped through the
17 lettered sacrificial mantras be pleased with me ( by giving His swarupa
jnanam)
The 17 lettered mantra is in Yajurveda
आश्रावयेति चतुरक्षरमस्तु श्रौषडिति चतुरक्षरं यजेति द्वयक्षरं ये यजामह इति
पञ्चाक्षरं यक्षरो वषटार (वौषट्) एष वै सप्तदशः
आश्रावयेति ( aa shraa va ya ) 4 letters आस्तु श्रौषडिति (a stu shrou shad )
4 letters
यजेति ( ya ja ) 2 letters ये यजामह इति ( ye ya jaa ma hey ) 5 letters वौषट्
( vou shat  ) 2 letters

Hope this helps.

regards
