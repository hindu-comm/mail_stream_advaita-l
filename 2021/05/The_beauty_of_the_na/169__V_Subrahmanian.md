The beauty of the name 'RAma' - Sri Thyagaraja expounds

In his composition 'Evarani....' Sri Thyagaraja says the two letters in the
name 'Ra-ma' denote the jiva akshara, seminal letters, in the Shiva mantra
- NamaH shivAya and the Narayana Mantra - Namo NarayaNAya -  'ma' and
'ra'.
