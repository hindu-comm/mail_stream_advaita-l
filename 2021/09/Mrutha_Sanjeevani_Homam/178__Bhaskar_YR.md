Hare Krishna

In Bengaluru, normally we do mrutasanjeevini homam ( mrutyunjaya homa / mahA mrutyunjaya homa) bit elaborately.  Gananpati pooja, punyAha, naandi praarthana, pancha gavya, kalasha sthApana ( if the kartru is smArtha then we do shiva panchAyatana) including kuladevata and  Navagraha.  With regard to pooja ritual we do, shOdashOpachAra pooja including paNchAmrutAbhishekam,  mahAnyAsa pUrvaka rudraabhishekam, rudra trishati pooja etc.  Then with regard to hOma ritual, Ganapati, Navagraha, pachaayatana, kula devata homa and then trayambaka mantra with beejaakshara we do homa (tila, vreehi aajya and amruta balli ( a type of stick/samit).  hOma saNkhya will be 1/10th of japa saNkhya.  

If the kartru is not smArta, then adhidevata brahma, pratyadhidevata vishNu sameta mahA mrutyunjaya kalasha sthApana ( lingatO Bhadra mandala pooja) and hOma we will do.  After poorNAhutu, kalasha visarjana, maarjana, durdaana, taila ghata avalOkana, phala pooja, Ashirvachana etc. will be there.  

The main purpose is to avoid accidental deaths ( akAlika mrutyu / apamrutyu) and deerghAyushyaM with good health.  

Hari Hari Hari Bol!!!
bhaskar

Don't know about Mrutha Sanjeevani homam, but Shanthi Kusumakaram and Shanthi Ratnakaram describe the vidhana of Maha Mrityunjaya homam prescribed by Bodhayana Maharishi and Saunaka Maharishi.
These are performed to ward off any ill effects on health.
To ensure that this homam to be fruitful, sampradayaika veda vidvans follows chanting of Gayatri MAntra japa.

Like for an example if the vidvans or griha acharya say that homa ahuti of
1000 to be done then
People who are coming for that homa including the grihasta will perform
1008 japa of Gayatri and
1000 japa of mantra that will be recited for the homa with proper sankalpa and then the homa is conducted.
