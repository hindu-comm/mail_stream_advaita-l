 Dear Sri Subramanian,
The following upanishad mantras reveal the same thought positionas told by the late pontiff of Shringeri . The mantras are as foloows :
ahamEvEdagaM sarvam ||Chandgya 7-25-1
AtmaivEdagm sarvavam || Chandgya 7-25-2
Atmata EvEdagAm sarvam ||Chandgya 7-26-1sarvagaM hyetad brahma | ayamAtmA brahma|| Mandukya Mantra 2
The above mantras reveal facts of life 
which can be cognized by one and all.So says Sri Shankara and declares that Sruti reveals existing facts only.
With respectful namaskars,Sreenivasa murthy
