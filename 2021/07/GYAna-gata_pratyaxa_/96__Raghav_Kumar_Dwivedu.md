> "When, if at all, is GYAnagata-pratyaxatvam condition met, even while

> 2. Is not the antaHkaraNa extending to viShaya seen in all cases of

>  viShayagata-pratyaxatva is met with. When condition of
>  viShayagata-pratyaxatva is  not met with, associated GYAna is categorized
> as parOxa.

> satisfy both the criteria of GYAnagata-pratyaxatvam and
> viShayagata-pratyaxatvaM? >>,
>
> This is explicitly answered in VP itself.  Sukha etc also satisfy both the
> criteria of GYAnagata-pratyaxatvam and viShayagata-pratyaxatvaM.
