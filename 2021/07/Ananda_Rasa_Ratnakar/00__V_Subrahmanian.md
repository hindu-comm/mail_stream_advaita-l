Brief about the book :

‘ Ananda Rasa Ratnakar ’ is a collection of Hindi writings of Maharajshree
Swami Akhandananda Saraswati (1911 – 1987), the great sage of Brindavan.
Compiled by his disciple, Swami Govindanda Saraswati, the book is a
collection of 366 articles, structured as a daily reader. On the persistent
request of devotees, an English translation of the book was decided to be
brought out, in four volumes. The current book is the first volume.
