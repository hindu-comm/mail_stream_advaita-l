Srimad Narayaneeyam Archanam

Gowrishankar ji has announced the Special Prayers of Narayaneeyam Archanam
for getting rid of the Pandemic and restoring peace, good health and
prosperity of people all across the globe.

On Aug 31st Tuesday starting  at 7.30 AM IST
Live Telecast on Sri Sankara TV.

7.30 AM Narayaneeyam Archanam Sri Sankara TV. Special Telecast.

3.30 PM DPS.

6.00 to 7.30  PM Srimad Bhagavata Saptaham.  (by Br Sri  Sriram
Ghanapathigal)
