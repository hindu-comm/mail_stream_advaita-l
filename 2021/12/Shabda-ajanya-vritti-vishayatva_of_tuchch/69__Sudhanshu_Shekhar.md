Venkataraghavan ji,

तुच्छं न क्षणिकम्, अकारणत्वात्, ब्रह्मवत्।

Here paksha is tuchcha, अक्षणिकत्व is sAdhya.

How can we hold that paksha is tuchcha shabda?

Regards
