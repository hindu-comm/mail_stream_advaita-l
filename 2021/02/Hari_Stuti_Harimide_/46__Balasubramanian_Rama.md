There’s a publication of this along with svayamprakAsha yati’s commentary.

N. Veezhinathan, “In Adoration of the Self, The Haristuti of Sankara with
the Commentary Haritattvamuktavali of Svayamprakasayati,” The Adi Sankara
Advaita Reserach Center, Chennai, 2001.

This has a detailed introduction, translation of the verses and the
commentary without translation.

Ramakrishnan
