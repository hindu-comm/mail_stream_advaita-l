> The deluded Jivas have desires, avidyA --> kAma --> karma is the cyclical
> triad. These desires can be good or bad. When the sum total of the good
> desires is ripe for countering the bad in the world, there is avatAra of
> Ishvara. The need is not for Advaita Vedanta itself, but as an answer to
> the prayers of the bhaktas, who are themselves helpless. Moreover, it is
> also for Advaita Vedanta as it is also part of Vedas, which need to be
> preserved by defeating the Asuras.
