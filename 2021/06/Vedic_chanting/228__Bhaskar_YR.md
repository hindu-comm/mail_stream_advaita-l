Hare Krishna

I don't know about others, when there is any mistake in pronunciation / svara during the chanting of veda mantra-s, we say three times nArAyaNa and continue the chanting after correcting the pronunciation / svara, definitely not from the beginning, starting from where the error happened.  And at the end anyway we chant yadakshara padabhrashtaM mAtrAheenantu yadbhavet etc. 

Hari Hari Hari Bol!!!
