Tiliheluva Neeti-kathegalu (Kannada) – Free download of e-book from Centre

This is a compilation in Kannada of the parables narrated by HH Sri
Abhinava Vidyateertha Mahaswamigalu, the 35th Jagadguru Shankaracharya of
Sri Sharada Peetham, Sringeri.

How to maintain composure even when stressed? Are desires harmful? Why no
contentment despite possessions? Is everything predestined? Can destiny be
overcome? Why do the innocents suffer and the wicked prosper? What
constitutes right and wrong? How to follow dharma, yet be pragmatic and
efficient? Who is the Guru to be sought after? How to avoid being misled by
fake gurus? Karma yoga – what, why and how? “The world is an illusion” –
what does this mean? What is the means to lasting happiness?

These and many such concerns are logically answered through parables
narrated by His Holiness.
