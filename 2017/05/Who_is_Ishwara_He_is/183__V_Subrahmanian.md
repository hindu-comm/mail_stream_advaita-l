Mostly those who omit that consciously are Vaishnavas, as evidenced by the
above Acharya.  I have heard from Sri Mani Dravid Sastrigal that in
Maharashtra (Pune) and other places in the north the reading with 'sa
Harih' is in vogue in many traditional pathashalas and lay persons
irrespective of whether they are vaishnavas or not.
