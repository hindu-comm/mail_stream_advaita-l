'Dahara vidyā prakāśa'

Sri Paramshivendra Sarswati (1600 - 1700), Guru of Sri Sadashiva
brahmendra, composed this work: 'Dahara vidya prakasha'. Download at:

http://www.dakshinamurtimathaprakashan.org/view.php?id=Dahar%
20vidihya%20Prakashika.pdf

A short work based on the Upanishadic Dahara vidya. He cites the Shānkara
bhāṣya and adds a number of Puranic verses. He has also cited from the
Soundarya lahari, upadeśa panchakam and other works of Shankara.
