> Dear Sriram,

> The first two verses are chanted daily during sandhyavandanam by Telugu
> and Kannada speaking  smārta-s.  A Telugu sandhyavandanam book by Krishna
> Pandita printed in the early 1900 s has these verses. Also these verses are
> present in the Mahabharata, in a slightly varied form, as stated by Arjuna.
>

Mahabharata:

शिवाय विष्णुरूपाय विष्णवे शिवरूपिणे ।।

दक्षयज्ञविनाशाय हरिरूपाय ते नमः। 3.39.76

In the Harivamsha, an extension of the Mahabharata, we have Makandeya's
words:

मार्कण्डेय उवाच॥ शिवाय विष्णुरूपाय विष्णवे शिवरूपिणे। अथान्तरं न पश्यामि
तेन ते दिशतः शिवम् ।
