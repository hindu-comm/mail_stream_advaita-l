1. Even the so many mails are also mithyaa

2. There is a story related to this in YogavaashishTa. When Rama heard from Sage Vashista that everything is mithyaa - he wanted to test the authentity of this statement. When Sage was coming to the Court, he sent an elephahant to chase him. Obviously Sage ran away to protect himself. After the incident - Rama questioned why he ran away since elephant is mithyaa. Sage replyed that - Rama even my running away is also mithyaa. 

Hari Om!Sadananda
