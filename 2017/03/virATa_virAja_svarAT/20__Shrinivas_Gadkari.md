Picking up a few month old thread. 
The mantra under discussion is 5th mantra on puruSa sUkta. 

Few interesting points should be noted: 
- Three different entities are mentioned here: puruSa, virATa, adhi puruSa 
- Following the commentary by sAyaNa: 
  puruSa = Adi puruSa 
  virATa = brahmANDa deha 
  adhi puruSa = paramAtmA 
- There is a clear mention of a trinity here: 
  puruSa = Father 
  adhi puruSa = Son 
  virATa = let us call this entity (divine) Mother 
- Labeling the entity identified with brahmANDa deha as ardhAngini of puruSa or 
  Adi mAtA (Mother) should be fine. In fact the word ardhAngini seems most
  appropriate here.
- Interestingly, it is paramAtmA (the Son) who is call vedAnta vedya - the one who 
  is to be understood via study of vedAnata. 

Hari Om.

Comments from people who have contemplated on these topics are welcome. 
Comments from people into shAkta upAsanA will be very useful.

Regards, 
Shrinivas Gadkari
