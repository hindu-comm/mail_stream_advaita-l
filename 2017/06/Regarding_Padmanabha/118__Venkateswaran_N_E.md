The is another beautiful Vishnu lying on Ananta, carved out of a monolith
rock at
Budha Nilakanta near Kathmandu, Nepal.

Here the Vishnu faces the sky and not lying one side as the south Indian
vigrahas.

This is believed to belong to 7th century CE, so earlier than Ramanuja.

Check Wikipedia for more details.

https://en.m.wikipedia.org/wiki/Budhanilkantha_Temple


Venkateswaran
