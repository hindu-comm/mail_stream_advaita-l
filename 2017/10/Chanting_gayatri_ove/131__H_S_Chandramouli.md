> It is clear,both from our own personal experience and the views expressed
> here, that practically all of us, those living here as well as those
> abroad, are violating in one way or another, fairly regularly, what is
> prescribed by the Shastras. There are always penalties associated with such
> violations. As Jagadguru Sri Chandrashekhara Bharati Swaminah of Sringeri
> has said, in the book “Dialogues with the Guru “,  it is a matter each
> person has to decide for himself –  Violate the rules prescribed in the
> shAstrAs for the sake of current benefits and suffer the penalties at a
> later time ( in this birth itself or in later births ), or follow the rules
> strictly even if it involves inconvenience at the present time and avoid
> penalties to be suffered at a later time.
