Dear Shakta Bandhu’s,



Sarva Siddhi Kruta stotram says

“Akachaaditatonnaddhapayashakshara vargineem

Jyeshtaamga baahu Padagramadhyastana nivaasineem”



Motherwalks in the path of ladder i.e Aa, Ka, Cha, Ta, Pa which are varga’s (described in serpent power by Sir John Woodroofe) & stays in the Bindu of43rd Triangle .



Lakshmi, goddess of wealth associated with today’s festival. One should pray for herassistance in cultivation, spiritual wealth, compassion, forgiveness &Loving kindness.



Letevery one of us illumine our hearts with the source of light & happinessfor all who comes in contact with us. One should love & forgiveun-conditionally and be in the alignment to receive cosmic abundance of love,light & happiness.



“LokaSamstha Sukihno Bhavantu”Ganapathi
