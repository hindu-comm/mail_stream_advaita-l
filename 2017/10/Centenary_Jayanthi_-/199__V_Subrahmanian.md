Sri Sharada Shankara bhakta mandali

On the Auspicious Occasion of the Birth Centennial of our Jyestha
Mahasannidhanam Jagadguru Sri Abhinava Vidyateertha Mahaswamigalu, GURU
puja Ashtottara shatanamavali parayana, Discourse ( Upanyasa), Prasada
viniyoga are arranged,
At : Sri Sringeri Shankara matha, Bangalore,
on Sunday 15 October 2017
from 10:30 am onwards

Since, Bengaluru is the Birth place (Janmabhoomi) of Jyestha
Mahasannidhanam, it is a great opportunity to the Bengaluru devotees to
serve the Jagadguru, by participating in this program.
