FRom  12th to 14th centuries, in the Telugu region there was Hariharanatha
cult. Later it was in Karnataka
and also  all over India. One hears of Hariharanatha temple in Sonepur in
Bihar

The second poet in Telugu Kavitraya, Tikkana Somayaji dedicated his
translation of Mahabharata
to Hariharanatha.

Bhattoji Dikshita the author of Sidhhantakaumudi and his nephew Kounda
Bhatta author of
Vaiakarana Bhushana Sara are devotees of Hariharanatha.

regards,
Sarma.
