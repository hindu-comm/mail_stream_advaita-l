that Tikkana dedicated his Mahabharata Telugu work (Andhra Bhāratamu) to
Hariharanatha of Nellore. It also says that by doing this Tikkana aimed at
the reconciliation of Vaiṣṇavas and Śaivas but did not succeed fully:
