 For that matter more than 250
 works (like bhAshya-s, prakaraNa-s, stOtra-s, upadesha,
 prashnOttara etc. ) have been popularly attributed to
 prasthAnatraya bhAshyakAra.
  It is all based on volumes of work ( I think it is about 20
 volumes) published by vANi vilas press, Srirangam. And
 according to some reliable sources, tradition would believe
 that all these works have been approved and accepted as the
 work of bhAshyakAra
  by the then Sringeri Jagadguru HH Sri sacchidaananda
 shivAbhinava nrusimha bhArati mahAswaminaH. But nowadays
 in scholarly circle all these works have not been considered
 as the work of bhAshyakAra hence during the siddhAnta
 pratipAdana most of the times
  they use only prasthAna traya bhAshya. It is only these
 works that will be treated as self-sufficient material to
 determine the Advaita siddhAnta as per Adi shankara. As
 far as my knowledge goes, no traditional Acharya doubted the
 authorship of prasthAna
  traya bhAshya though there is strong enough doubts on the
 authorship of viveka chudAmani, trishati bhAshya,
 prapanchasaara, soundarya lahari etc. And now you are
 debating that even geeta bhAshya too not from Adi
 shankaraâ€™s pen and trying to make prasthAna
  traya to prasthAna dvaya J

 Dear friends,



 Regarding the total 16 Shankarabhashyas, we can make it
 easier if we take that 12 bhashyas (i.e. the
 Bhagavadgiabhasya + Brahmasutrbhashya + the ten major
 Upanishad bhshyas) as confirmed.




 Then, we have to fill in only four remaining places with
 four Shankara bhashyas out of the following 7 bhashyas...If
 I have left out any other contender(s), you may kindly add
 those too, to these seven contenders.




 1)

 Nrsimhapurvatapaiyaupanishad bhashy

 2)

 Shvetasvataraupanishad bhashys

 3

 )Kenopanishad vakyabhashya

 4)

 Lalitatrishati bhashya

 5)

 Sanatsujatiya bhashya

 6)

 Yogasutravivaranabhshya

 7)

 Vishnusahasranama bhashya



 I shall be thankful if you can kindly come out with your
 suggestions. 



 Regards,

 Sunil KB
