 Sri Gurudhyanam composed by Jagadguru Sri Sri Bharati Tirtha Mahaswamiji

॥ श्री गुरुध्यानम् ॥

अज्ञानध्वान्तविध्वंसिसहस्रकरतेजसम् ।
सच्चित्सुखात्मकब्रह्मनिविष्टहृदयाम्बुजम् ॥ १ ॥

पद्मासनोपविष्टञ्च ध्यानस्तिमितलोचनम् ।
समं कायशिरोग्रीवं धारयन्तं महाप्रभम् ॥ २ ॥

सहस्रकिरणस्पर्धिदेहकान्तिसमुज्ज्वलम् ।
निर्विकल्पसमाधिस्थं निश्चलावयवं शिवम् ॥ ३ ॥

देहादिब्रह्मपर्यन्ते भोग्ये तुच्छत्वधीयुतम् ।
आर्तानामार्तिहन्तारं जिज्ञासुविशयच्छिदम् ॥ ४ ॥

स्वावलोकनमात्रेण पुनानं निखिलं जगत् ।
मदीयहृत्सरोजातनिवेशितपदद्वयम् ।
विद्यातीर्थगुरूत्तंसं ध्यायामि भवमुक्तये ॥ ५ ॥

॥ śrī gurudhyānam ॥

ajñānadhvāntavidhvaṁsisahasrakaratējasam ।
saccitsukhātmakabrahmaniviṣṭahr̥dayāmbujam ॥ 1 ॥

padmāsanōpaviṣṭañca dhyānastimitalōcanam ।
samaṁ kāyaśirōgrīvaṁ dhārayantaṁ mahāprabham ॥ 2 ॥

sahasrakiraṇaspardhidēhakāntisamujjvalam ।
nirvikalpasamādhisthaṁ niścalāvayavaṁ śivam ॥ 3 ॥

dēhādibrahmaparyantē bhōgyē tucchatvadhīyutam ।
ārtānāmārtihantāraṁ jijñāsuviśayacchidam ॥ 4 ॥

svāvalōkanamātrēṇa punānaṁ nikhilaṁ jagat ।
madīyahr̥tsarōjātanivēśitapadadvayam ।
vidyātīrthagurūttaṁsaṁ dhyāyāmi bhavamuktayē ॥ 5 ॥

Translation

1. Resplendent like the thousand-rayed Sun, dispelling the darkness of
ignorance, with mind fixed on Brahman, the Guru is the embodiment of
existence-consciousness-bliss.

2. Seated in Padmasana and in deep meditation with eyes still, holding his
body, neck, and head upright, He shines brilliantly

3. With bodily splendour rivaling that of the Sun, seated in Nirvikalpa
Samadhi, with limbs absolutely still, my auspicious Guru shines.

4. Considering objects of enjoyment starting from Lord Brahma to this body
as thoroughly insignificant, He puts an end to the miseries of the
afflicted and the doubts of those desiring of knowledge.

5. I meditate on the foremost Guru Vidyatirtha for liberation from samsara.
He purifies the world with His glance, and has placed His two feet in the
lotus of my heart.
