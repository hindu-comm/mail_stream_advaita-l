> >
> > The following verse is from Madhaviya Shankara Vijaya, Chapter 6, verse
> > 41. Context is when Siva  as an Out-caste confronts Sri Bhagavatpada.
> The
> > verse is part of the response by Sri Bhagavatpada.
> >
> >
> >
> > <<  दासस्तेहं  देहदृष्ट्याऽस्मि  शंमभो
> >
> > जातस्तेऽंशो  जीवदृृष्ट्या त्रिदृष्टे  ॥
> >
> > सर्वस्याऽऽत्मन्नात्मदृष्ट्या  त्वमेवे-
> >
> > त्येवं  मे  धीर्निश्चिता  सर्वशास्त्रैः  ॥ >>
> >
> >
> >
> > << dAsastehaM  dehadRRiShTyA.asmi  shaMmabho
> >
> > jAtasteM.asho  jIvadRRiRRiShTyA tridRRiShTe  ||
> >
> > sarvasyA.a.atmannAtmadRRiShTyA  tvameve-
> >
> > tyevaM  me  dhIrnishchitA  sarvashAstraiH  || >>
> >
> >
> >
> > Translation by Swami Tapasyananda (Sri Ramakrishna Mutt)  << I am thy
> > servant when I am conscious of myself as the body. I am Thy part, O
> > Three-eyed one, when the awareness of Jiva dawns on me… And when the
> Atman
> > consciousness becomes established, I recognize myself as one with Thee.
> > Such, indeed, is the teaching of all scriptures. >>
