> Namaste Subbuji,

> Thanks, this reminds me of an incident I was told about Shridhar Swamiji by
> someone who frequently accompanied Him. Swamiji was then recently gifted a
> tiger skin by someone and was sitting on it in a similar set up of darshana
> when a person was waiting in front of Swamiji for long. When the person was
> asked by Swamiji as to if he wanted something, he said he wanted a tiger
> skin. Swamiji asked someone to look inside if there was any tiger skin to
> be found but there was none. This person was eyeing Swamiji's seat with the
> tiger's head nicely filled up and looking alive. Then, Sridhar Swamiji
> asked "idu kodlano? (shall I give you this)?" pointing to it. He said
> "yes". Swamiji just got up and handed over the same. The chap went away.
>
> A day later or so, in the darshana, Swamiji asked around for the person who
> had gifted the tiger skin, as he was nowhere to be found. On inquiring,
> someone said, the person hasn't come. Swamiji said, "hmmm, lets go to his
> place"! On reaching his house, the person was surprised seeing the
> visitors. Swamiji smilingly asked him "yAko, nanna mele kopA bandidiyA?
> (why, are you angry with me)"? He started crying replying "No Swamiji, I
> felt sad that you gave away the tiger skin which I had prepared for you. It
> was for you to use!" On this Swamiji said the golden words "eshto adu mohA
> hulicharmada mele!!! (how much is the attachment you have to the tiger
> skin)? After you gave it to me, was it yours to cry over?" Suddenly, the
> fellow fell at Swamiji's feet and expressed his wish to perform pAdapUja.
> He didn't have much. Swamiji just asked him to not bother for details, but
> just put a flower and pour water on the feet.
