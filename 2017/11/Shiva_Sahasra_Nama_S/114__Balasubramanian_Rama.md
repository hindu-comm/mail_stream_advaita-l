A good reference book is Ramkaran Sharma, "Sivasahasranamastakam: Eight
collections of hymns containing one thousand and eight names of Siva", Nag
Publishers. It contains a detailed exposition about the text from the
Mahabharata and comparing versions of it in various Puranas.

Rama
