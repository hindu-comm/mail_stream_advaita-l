Namaste

Continuing in the Naama Sankeertana stotras of Adi Sankara he has written
one tailor made for Shiva Naama Sankeertana. This is Shiva Naamaavali
Ashtakam.

In this Stotra every Sloka in the first eight ends with
Samsaaraduhkhagahanaajjagadeesha Raksha but the last one ends with
दारिद्र्यदुःखदहनाय नमः शिवाय like Daaridrya Duhkha Dahana Stotram.
