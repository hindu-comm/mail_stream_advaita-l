A Commentary on Prashnottara Ratnamaalika of Sri Adi Shankaracharya, by Sri
S.S. Naganand, is a translation and commentary on the "necklace of gems,"
of Sri Adi Shankaracharya, which is a series of question and answer style
verses on Sri Adishankaracharya's teachings.

It is a series of shlokas which convey philosophical truths propounded by
Sri Adi Shankaracharya, the Founder of the Advaita school of Hindu
Philosophy. Sri S.S.

Naganand has made the teachings of Sri Adi Shankaracharya accessible to one
and all with this thought provoking commentary giving a deep insight into
the life and work of the propounder of the Advaitha school of philosophy.
