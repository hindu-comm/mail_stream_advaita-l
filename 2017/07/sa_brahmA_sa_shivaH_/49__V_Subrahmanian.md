In the narayana sukta at the end we have the mantra:

'sa brahmA sa shivaH sa hariH sendraH..'

Similarly in the Nrsimha PurvatApinyupanishat too there is this line:

'sa brahmA sa shivaH sa hariH sendraH..'

The kaivalyopanishad has the line:

sa brahmA sa shivah, sendrah soksharah...sa eva vishnuh....

Are there any other sources where the above or similar expressions as a
string occur?
