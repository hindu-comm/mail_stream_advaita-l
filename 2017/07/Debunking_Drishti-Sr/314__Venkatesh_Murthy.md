DSV EJV is the main Advaita theory. Others are for preliminary study only.
Gaudapada taught that and Adi Sankara also taught that. Nowhere they have
said we require Aneka Jeeva Vaada. Bhamati said that but because of Mandana
Misra.

Difference between Waking and Dream states is there for immature people
thinking in the waking state 'This world is different and I am different'.
It is really not different. When he will understand the sameness of the two
he will be on the right path.
