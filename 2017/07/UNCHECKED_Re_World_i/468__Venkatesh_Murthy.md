Namaste

In Yoga Vasistha it is said Mano Manana Nirmaana Maatrameva Jagat Trayam.
Mind's Thoughts = Universe. The entire Universe is only a Thought in the
Mind of Saadhaka. What is Mind? It is a product of Avidyaa. Therefore this
Universe is only because of Avidyaa and it is not real.
