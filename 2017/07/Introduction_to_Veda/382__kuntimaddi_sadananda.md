Just released:
Hari Om!Sadananda-------------------------------------------------
Introduction to Vedanta – By Dr. K. Sadananda

https://www.amazon.com/dp/0999170406/ref=sr_1_2?s=books&ie=UTF8&qid=1500425363&sr=1-2&keywords=introduction+to+vedanta

This book starts with the fundamental human problemsand questions and search for happiness, and introduces the philosophy behindthe spirituality of the many Upanishads and the Bhagavad Gita, and providesbrief quotes from them. It goes on to explain the nature of the mind, itsclassification, and describes what this “I” is. In the process, it alsoexplains the errors in perception, what is real and what is unreal, and thenature of consciousness and ultimate reality. A brief list of materials forfurther exploration appears at the end of the book.
