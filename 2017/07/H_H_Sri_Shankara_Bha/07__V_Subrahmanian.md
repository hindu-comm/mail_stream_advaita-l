In preparation for Chaturmasya, the PURA PRAVESHA of HH Yadathore Sri Sri
Shankara Bharati Swamiji will be at 5 PM on THURSDAY 6th July 2017.

All devotees are requested to assemble 30 minutes in advance ( by 4.30pm)
near Sri Shivaganga Math III main, Chamarajpet Bengaluru-18. You may bring
Deepas for Aarathi to welcome HH.
Dhooli padapooja and Asheervachana follows.

Please attend this divine event in large numbers.
