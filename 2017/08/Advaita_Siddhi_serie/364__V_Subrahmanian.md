Though as a norm Advaitasiddhi is not taught to all those who are taught
the prasthana traya bhashyas, still there are special opportunities for
ardent students to study the work, guided by Vidwans of high order, and be
tested in the famous Tenali exams.

> sadness that their works are not easily understood by followers of the
> advaita school in general. In the words of MahamahopAdhyAya Shri Ganganath
> Jha, the three works - KhaNDana-khaNDa-khAdya, advaitasiddhi, and

> of intelligence" finds it difficult to reach, in the language of the
> pAThashAlas. Perhaps, with the encouragement and training provided by the
> likes of the Acharyas of Chennai, the day is not far off when any student
> of advaita interested in gaining proficiency in these difficult texts will
> have the means to do so, just as students of dvaita today can gain
> proficiency in nyAyasudha through training that has been made accessible,
> for example.
