> Page 6, para 7, last few lines.  I am translating it here.
>
>
>
> << But Vedanta Sidhanta states that only atman is satya, rest is all
> anruta/mithya/appearences perceived by people having wrong knowledge >>
>
>
>
> << anAtma, which appears in some states subservient to Atman who is
> jnAnaswarUpa, is asatya/mithyA/anruta. >>.
>
>
>
> << “  anruta is mithya “ does not mean that it does not appear at all. It
> is not different from Atman. It does not have independent Existence. Looked
> at carefully, it is Atman only. This is the meaning.  This should always be
> borne in mind when encountering terms like mithyA/anruta etc. >>.
