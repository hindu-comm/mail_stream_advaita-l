In BSB 3.2.4 Shankara says that in the dream, the objects are all created
by the jiva only. And states a difference between the jiva's creation in
dream and the objects of the waking. Also, he says: Even the creation of
the world of waking is not ultimately real, as has been established in BSB
2.1.14 that the entire world is māyāmātram.
