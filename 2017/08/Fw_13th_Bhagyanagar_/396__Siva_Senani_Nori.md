The Nori Charitable Trust welcomes all believers in Veda to the 13th Bhagyanagar Veda Sabha that wil be conducted at Sankara Matham, Nallakunta, Hyderabad from 7:30 am to 7:30 pm on 3rd September, 2017, Sunday. Around forty Ghanapathins and Kramapathins from Telangana and AP are expected to participate. The format would be the Pareekshaa paddhati, where over twelve rounds, the assembled scholars continue the recitation started by the President and Vice-president of the Sabha at random points.
Results of the test conducted on 2nd September, Saturday for new Vedic scholars would also be announced on the day.
Lunch would be served at the venue.
RegardsN. Siva Senani
