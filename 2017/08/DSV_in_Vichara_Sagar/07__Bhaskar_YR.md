Outside the dream there is no Ishwara and no Jeeva

praNAms
Hare Krishna

Yes, outside the dream neither Ishwara nor jeeva before the creation of long lasting dream what was there was one without second that which thought about becoming many ‘sO kAmayata bahusyAm prajAyeyeti…yatO vA imAni bhUtAni jAyante yena jAtAni jeevanti ….tadvijijnAsasva tad brahmeti…says shruti.. The deergha svapna kartru is nothing but that which ekamevAdviteeyaM.
