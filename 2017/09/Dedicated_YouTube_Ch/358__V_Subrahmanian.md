Dedicated YouTube Channel for Sadhu Om

Eminent musician Sriram Parthasarathy has launched a YouTube channel
dedicated to the compositions of Sadhu Om who lived in the presence of
Bhagavan Ramana Maharshi.

http://sadhuom.net/


The songs, in Tamil , are full of Vedantic import. Siram has committed
himself to bring out the deep message of the compositions through the
medium of voice and music. The very first pieces are very impressive:

https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=c0rMTPuHh9k

Subscribe to the channel and get to see more and more additions to this
drive.
