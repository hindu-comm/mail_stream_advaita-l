Shankara says here: The rajju-sarpa that is produced by avidyAkRuta
mAyAbeeja, has for its existence the rajju alone. In other words, he says,
the sarpa, prior to its utpatti, exists in the rajju 'as rajju' alone. So
too, before the world of manifoldness is manifested, it exists as
'prāṇabeejātmā' (Brahman with the creator upādhi). And he gives the spider
example from the Br.up.  That way, the satkāryavāda is protected. This is
in perfect agreement with the Taittiriya passages/method cited.
