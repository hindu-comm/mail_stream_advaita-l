>
> Swami Vasudeva Brahmendra Saraswati, in his Sanskrit commentary on Vichara
> Sagara, has this to say.
>
>
>
> Context is अहं  ब्रह्म ( ahaM  brahma ).
>
>
>
> Topic 198, book page 110, adobe page 213.
>



> << अहंशब्दस्य  द्वावर्थौ ;  तत्र  कूटस्थस्य  ब्रह्मणा  मुख्यसामानाधिकरण्यं,
> चिदाभासस्य  बाधायां  सामानाधिकरण्यं  च  । >>,
>
>
>
> << ahaMshabdasya  dvAvarthau ;  tatra  kUTasthasya  brahmaNA
> mukhyasAmAnAdhikaraNyaM,  chidAbhAsasya  bAdhAyAM  sAmAnAdhikaraNyaM  cha  |
> >>,
>
>
>
> The issue is elaborated in the succeeding portions of the topic. SVBS does
> not cover  ऐक्यसामानाधिकरण्यं  (aikyasAmAnAdhikaraNyaM ) in the text.
>


    In the kannada commentary on Vichara Sagara by Swami Shankarananda, in
this part of the book, mind is also mentioned as alternative to chidAbhAsa
under bAdhAyAM  sAmAnAdhikaraNyaM.
