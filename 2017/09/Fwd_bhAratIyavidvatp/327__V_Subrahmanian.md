> As far as I know there is no collected works of Vachaspati Mishra but all
> of them are readily available as he has the notable distinction of being an
> authoratatiive acharya in five different darshanas.  His known works are
>
> samkhyatattvakaumudi on Samkhya karika of Ishvarakrishna (Samkhya)
>
> samkhyatattvavaishradi on samkhyapravachanabhashya of Vyasa on yogasutras
> of Patanjali. (Yoga)
>
> tatparyatika on nyayabhashyavartikka of Uddyotakara on nyayabhashya of
> Pakshilaswami Vatsyayana on nyayasutras of Gotama. (Nyaya)
>
> bhamati on sharirakamimamsabhashya of Shankaracharya on brahmasutras of
> Badarayana (Advaita Vedanta)
>
> brahmatattvasamiksha on Brahmasiddhi of Mandana Mishra (Non-Shankaran
> Advaita Vedanta,)
>
> nyayakanika on vidhiviveka of Mandana Mishra (Purva Mimamsa)
>
> tattvabindu an original work on Purva Mimamsa
>
> nyayasuchinibandha an original  work that establishes the proper number of
> sutras in the nyayasutras,
>
> All of these are readily available in the usual places such as DLI.
>
> There is also a 15th century "Abhinava" Vachaspati Mishra who wrote on
> dharmashastra who sometimes gets confused with his namesake.
