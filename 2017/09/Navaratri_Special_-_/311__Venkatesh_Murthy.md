Namaste

Mysore Maharaja Jayachamaraja Wodeyar was a Sri Vidya Upasaka. He composed
many beautiful Sanskrit songs in many Raagas. The specialty of the songs is
the name of the Raaga is in the song and the 'Ankita' is 'Sri Vidya'. In
many songs he has said words like Parama Advaita Marga Rata, Parama Advaita
Bodhaka, Parama Advaita Tattwa Priyaam, Akhanda Advaita Tattwa Prakashini
and so on.
