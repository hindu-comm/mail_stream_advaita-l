SRI SRINGERI SHARADA PEETAM - FOSTERING A TIMELESS PHILOSOPHY

The Hindu brings out Sri Sharada Peetam: Fostering a Timeless Philosophy, a
special volume that pays tribute to the Peetam's efforts to keep the flame
of Advaita Vedanta alive. Adi Sankara is believed to have set up the Peetam
and the Sharadamba Temple in what is now Karnataka on the banks of the
river Tunga. A significant set of photographs, including from The Hindu's
Photo Archives, presents the story graphically in this volume. (Cover Price
