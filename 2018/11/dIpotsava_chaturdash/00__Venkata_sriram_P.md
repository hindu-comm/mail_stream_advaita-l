Dear All,

A gentle reminder to all of you that this year दीपोत्सव चतुर्दशी falls on 6th Nov'18.  
Interesting, 6th is Tuesday and hence celebrated also as कृष्णाङ्गार चतुर्दशी. 

Those who know the method of यम तर्पणम् should perform this on 6th Nov'18.
