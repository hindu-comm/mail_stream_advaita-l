> धर्म-सेवा करना है, परन्तु तमोगुण से रजोगुण और रजोगुण से सत्वगुण श्रेष्ठ है
> ।
>
> We know there are four Purusharthas: Dharma, Artha, Kama and Moksha.
> Manusmriti says: Kaama (desire / pleasure seeking) is tamoguna-driven,
> Artha (wealth-seeking) is rajo-guna and Dharma, righteousness,is
> sattva-guna.  Moksha, therefore, is transcending all three gunas. There is
> also the exhortation that 'artha and kaama have to be based on dharma, when
> alone the two, artha and kaama, will be untainted and will be conducive to
> moksha.
