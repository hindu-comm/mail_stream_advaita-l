> Anatman derives its attributes of existence, knowability (knowledge ),
> capacity to know  etc from the Atman by its mere proximity to it. This is
> how “ transfer “ of attributes from atman to anatman is to be understood.
> The “ transfer “ is not an act on the part of atman. Illustration is that
> of rope-serpent. The rope-serpent derives its existence and knowability
> from the rope. Rope has no active role to play in such “ transfer “ of
> attributes. But the anatma is not aware of where it got its attributes
> from. It needs sadhana to know it.
