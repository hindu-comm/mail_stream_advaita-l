PraNAms to all.
We are doing the Panchadasi Ch. 6 -Chitadeepa prakaraNa.In that, there is a discussion adhyaasa using ghaTaakaasa, jalaakaasha, meghaakaasha and mahaakaasha in relation to kuuTashta, brahman, jeeva and Iswara. How do you explainthese analogies and more importantly the anyonya adhyaasahere?
Panchadasi- Ch. 6 – Sloka24 says:
Jalavyomnaa ghaTaakaaShO yathaa sarvastirohitaH|

Tathaa jeevena kuuTasthaH saH anyOnya adhyaasa uchyate||

yathaa, Jalavyomnaa ghaTaakaaShaH, sarvaH tirOhitaH; tathaa jeevena kuuTasthaH, saH anyonya adhyaasaH
It says, anyonya adhhaasa – what is the mutual superimposition here?
Hari Om!Sadananda
