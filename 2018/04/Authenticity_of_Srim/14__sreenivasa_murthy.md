Dear friends, It is immaterial whether Srimad Bhagavatam is authentic or not. What is important to consisder is whether what has been taught in it is authentic or not. That is all. It will be more profittable and fruitful if one engages himself in finding out about the authenticity of the teachings contained in it.
With respectful namaskars,Sreenivasa Murthy.
