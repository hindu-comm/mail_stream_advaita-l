> काश्मीरमण्डलं also has a parvata called श्रीशैल.  Also, the dhyAna shloka
> has a word 'शान्डिल्येनोपनीता' ie., that which has been brought into
> presence by the penance of Rishi Shandilya which is none other than the
> Sharada of Kashmir which is the result of the penance of Sage.
>
> Also, the name शारदा denotes that Goddess which is worshipped in the
> sharad (Autumn) that denotes the sharada navaratri mahapuja and hence the
> name shAradA.
