The name शारदा is derived as:

शरत्काले यतो देवी पूजिता सर्वजन्तुभिः ।
ततः प्रोक्ता पुराविद्भिः शारदा नामनामतः ॥

Which is none other than Durga.  However, as per shAradA mAhAtmyaM, the shArada in Kashmir assumed 3 forms to bless the Sage Shandilya namely shAradA, nAradA, vAgdevi.
