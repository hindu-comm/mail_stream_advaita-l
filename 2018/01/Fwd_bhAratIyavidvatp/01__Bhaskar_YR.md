Hare Krishna

In Kannada nitya devataarchana vidhi, published by Sri Vishnu sahasranaama satsangha sabha there is a collection of niraajana mantra-s and mantra pushpa mantra-s (mantra-s recited after niraajana).  And in shiva paNchayatana Pooja paddhati, Sringeri publication too you will find collection of these mantra-s.  

Hari Hari Hari Bol!!!
bhaskar
