>
> Svayamaprakasha yati also known as Svayamaprakasha Yogindra was a prolific
> commentator on Advaitic works who flourished around the beginning of the
> 17th century.  He was the shishya of Kaivalyananda Yogindra.
>
> Not only the Dakshinamurti stotra, he has also commented on a number of
> prakaranas of Shankaracharya: ekashloki, atmanatmaviveka, mohamudgara
> (bhaja govindam.  This one is notable because the order and number of the
> verses diverges markedly from the standard text), and haristuti.
>
>   He has also commented on other advaita acharyas: for
> instance a tika on Vachaspati Mishras Bhamati on Shankaracharyas
> brahmasutrabhashya and a tika on advaitamakaranda of Lakshmidhara Kavi.
