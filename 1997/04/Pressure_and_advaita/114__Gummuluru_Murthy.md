On Tue, 29 Apr 1997, Neeta Pasrija wrote:

>
> Is the battle outside or inside us???
>

I think it is a very important statement.

Recognize that all the battles are inside us. There is nothing outside.
These battles are caused in the mind. Quieten the mind. Cut down the
fluctuations of the mind. You are a yogi. You will recognize you are
immortal and you are Brahman.

Regards
Gummuluru Murthy
