An Advaita-friendly selection from NilakanTha-s commentary to the SSS

Nama of the Mahabharata we get a glimpse of several seimnal Vedantic
concepts along with a treat of Hari-Hara abheda.  Citations from Shankara's
bhashyam is also provided in a few places. The file can be downloaded at:
