" Viveka Deepini " is a collection of Shlokas from Prashnottara Ratna
Malika of Jagadguru Adi Shankaracharya. Vedanta Bharati has already
published the book on Viveka Deepini in English written by Shri
S.S.Nagananda, who is also Vedanta Bharati's trustee. Now under the
supervision and guidance of Shri Nagananda, the same book has been
translated to 10 Indian Languages. These books will be released by the
Honorable Vice President of India Shri. M Venkaiah Naidu on Sunday 07th
July 2019  from 10:30AM @ Sri Satya Sai International Center, Bhishma
Pitamaha Road, Pragati vihar, Delhi. HH Sri Sri Shankara Bharati Swamaiji
who is pratron of Vedanta Bharati and pontiff of Sri Yoganandeshwara
Saraswati Mutt, KRNagar Mysore would grace this event.

*Along with this event, lectures on the importance of Adi Shankaracharya's
Stotras have been planned on  06th July evening in more than 20 centers
across Delhi. Center, Timing etc Details available at
 http://www.vedantabharati.org <http://www.vedantabharati.org>*
