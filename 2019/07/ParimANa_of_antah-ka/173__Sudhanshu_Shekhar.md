Hari Om,

What is held to be the parimANa of antah-karaNa in advaita siddhAnta? From
Brahma Sutra BhAshya 2.3.19-33, it appears that the same is stated to be
aNu parimANa.

Question arises as to how can antah-karaNa with aNu parimANa have vritti
like ghatAkAra-vritti etc?

Regards,
Sudhanshu.
