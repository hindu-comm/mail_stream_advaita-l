Dear friends,What are the essential qualifications required by a 
 student of Vedanta ?(1)Sri Shankara writes : 
ahaM ca nityEna amRutEna aBayEna kUtasthEna 
acalEna dhruvENa arthEna arthI na tadviparItEna || 
 (commentary to mantra 1-2-12 of MunDaka Upanishad.)(2) vidyABIpsinaM manyE nacikEtasaM || [Kathopanishad; Mantra 1-2-4](3) What is vidyA ? Sri Shankara writes : nirvikArAtmabuddhiSca vidyEtIha prakIrtita || [Upadeshasahasri; chapter 1 Shloka 12]The above teachings are self-explanatory. 
With respectful namaskars,Sreenivasa Murthy
