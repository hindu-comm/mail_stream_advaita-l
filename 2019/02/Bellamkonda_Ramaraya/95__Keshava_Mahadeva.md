Bellamkonda Ramaraya Kavindrulu was born as a Vishistaadvaitin, but in his
later became Advaita. It is said that Lord Hayagriva himself has graced
him. He has written various works refuting the philosophy of Ramanuja's
Vishistaadvaitam and supporting Advaita.

Below is a search of his name in archive.org. All his books with his name
are available:
