A unique verse of Vachaspati Misra invoking five deities

Here the invocation of various deities by Vachaspati Misra (841 CE) is
given along with an explanatory gloss: The deities are: Shiva, Aditya,
Ganapati, Gowri (non-different from Saraswati) and Vishnu.
