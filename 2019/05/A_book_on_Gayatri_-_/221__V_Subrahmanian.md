A book on 'Gayatri' - Kannada - by Vidwan Ganesh Bhat Hobli

Here are some details about a new book authored by well known scholar Sri
Ganesh Bhat Hobli, Bengaluru.

The book is a product of his larger discourse on Sri Lalita Sahasra Nama.
The names Gayatri, Vyahruti and Sandhya occur there. Upon being urged by
some persons to write on these names, the author came out with this book of
100 plus pages in Kannada.

The book has a number of valuable quotations from the scriptural literature
and added hymns too related to Gayatri, Surya, etc.

It is published by the Gokhale Institute of Public Affairs, Bangalore.
Price: Rs.100.  It is mentioned that the distributors and sellers are:
Sahitya Prakashana, Koppikar Street, Hubballi 580020.

One may also enquire with the GIPA, Bull Temple, Road, N R Colony,
Bangalore 560019.
