(Late) Mahamahopadhyaya Sri Ranganatha Sharma was a great scholar of
Vyakarana. He was a devoted admirer of Shankara Bhagavatpada. He had
co-penned the dialogues to the famous Sanskrit movie on Shankaracharya by
G.V.Iyer. He has authored, translated, etc. may works of and on Shankara
Bhagavatpada in Sanskrit and Kannada. The website is a great tribute to the
esteemed scholar. Most written material on the website is in Kannada.
