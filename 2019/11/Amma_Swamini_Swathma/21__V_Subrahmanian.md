In the Jivan Mukti Viveka, Swami Vidyaranya has said that sannyasa for
women is permitted. The 'praisha' mantra they have to utter is to be in
their native language.  The Mahabharata talks of a sannyasini by name
Sulabha whom Shankara has cited in the Brahmasutra bhashya:

अथ धर्मयुगे तस्मिन यॊगधर्मम अनुष्ठिता महीम अनुचचारैका सुलभा नाम भिक्षुकी 8
 https://www.sacred-texts.com/hin/mbs/mbs12308.htm
