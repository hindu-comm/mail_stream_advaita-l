True. It seems that when He was taking classes, he would not impose stringent rules as to who should attend and who should not. He would say 'For the IAS, many thousands write the exam and a much less number make it to the interview and only the rare few  finally get selected. In the same way, even if a few attain to the knowledge of the truth finally by attending the classes, that is certainly laudable.'

>  manushyANAm sahasreshu ...vettu tattvataH, expresses the same opinion by lord in Geeta...
