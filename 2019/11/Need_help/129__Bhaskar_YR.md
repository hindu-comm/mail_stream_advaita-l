Since bhUta pishAcha etc. in the army of bhUta gaNa the big boss is 'bhUtanAtha' and his better half is pishAchini (namaH pishAchini karaNkini trishUla khadga haste etc.) he or she will take care if there is any mischievous activity from pishachi.  But, to be on the safer side,  it is always better to wear 'dushta shakti nibarhaNa yaNtra' before reading anything about bhUta, preta, pishachi üòä üòä
