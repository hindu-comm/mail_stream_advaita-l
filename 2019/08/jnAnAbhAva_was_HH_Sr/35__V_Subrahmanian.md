There is a verse in the Vivekachudamani that shows what constitute
aparoksha and what paroksha jnanam:

बन्धो मोक्षश्च तृप्तिश्च चिन्तारोग्यक्षुदादयः ।
स्वेनैव वेद्या यज्ज्ञानं परेषामानुमानिकम् ॥
bandho mokṣaśca tṛptiśca cintārogyakṣudādayaḥ |
svenaiva vedyā yajjñānaṃ pareṣāmānumānikam ||
(Sankaracarya's Vivekachudamani 476)

Bondage and liberation, contentment and anxiety, health, hunger, etc. are
directly known only by the person concerned; others have knowledge of these
by mere inference.
