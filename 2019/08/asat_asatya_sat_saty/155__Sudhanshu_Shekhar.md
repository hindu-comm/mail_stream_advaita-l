> Asat and mithya are non-existence as per Swamiji. World is Brahman only.
> Its upAdAna is Maya which is non-different from Brahman. However, due to
> avidya, we imagine this world as different from Brahman. This
> different-from-Brahman-imagined-world is asat/mithya. Similarly Maya is
> non-different-from-Brahman. But because of avidya, we imagine Maya to be
> different from Brahman. Such different-from-Brahman-imagined-Maya is asat
> like shasha-vishana and mithya. Such Maya is stated to be non-existent and
> not the one which is non-different from Brahman.
>
>
>
> Further, satya and asatya are both sat. Asat is non-existence. World is
> asatya and sat. Brahman is sat and satya (satya refers to sad-Brahman of
> sadeva saumya). Asatya is not sat and hence neither satya nor asatya. This
> is what Swamiji holds as per Bhashya.
