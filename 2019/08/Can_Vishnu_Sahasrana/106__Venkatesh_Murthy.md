I had one more question. Can we say Nakshatra Sukta in the day time?
Nakshatras are not visible in day time. Should we chant Nakshatra Mantras
in the night only?

What about Nava Graha Mantras? Except Aditya all Grahas are fully visible
in the night only or before sunrise or after sunset.
