BHASHYAMRITAVAAHINI

Taittareeya Upanishat Shaankara Bhaasya Pravachanamalika will be held in
various centers all over Chikkamagaluru district from 18th to 23rd March.

Jagadguru Sri Vidhushekhara Bharathi Sannidhana will inaugurate the Saptaha
at Sringeri on 18th March.
The Valedictory program is held at 3:30pm on 24th March at Pravachana
Mandira, Sringeri Sharada Peetham, in the Divine presence of Jagadguru Sri
Bharati Teertha Mahasannidhana & Jagadguru Sri Vidhushekhara Bharati
Sannidhana.
Sri Shankara Bharati Swamiji will also be present.
