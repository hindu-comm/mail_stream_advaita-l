Dear readers,
 What is the true svarUpa of Jnani?
Sri Shankara says :(1) viduShaH AtmatvAt|| [commentary to shloka 2-21 of Bhagavadgita](2) sa ESha vidvAn sarvOpAdhiH sarvAtmA sarvO Bavati | nirupAdhi - nirupAKyO - anantarO - abAhyaH kRutsnaH 
 prajnAnaGanaH ajaH amaraH aBayaH amRutaH acalaH 
 nEtinEti, asthulaH anaNuH - ityEvaM viSEShaNO Bavati || [commentary to mantra 2-5-15 of Bruhadaranyaka upanishad](3) vipaScit mEdhAvI, avipariluptacaitanyasvaBAvatvAt || [commentary to mantra 1-2-18 of Katha upanishad]In the light of the above teachings are the notions 
which are commonly prevailing in the present times 
 correct or incorrect? I do not know.
The scholars and the readers should decide.With respectful namaskars,Sreenivasa Murthy
