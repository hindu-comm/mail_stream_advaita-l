The text describes three manifestations ofAdi mAtA:1. gAyatrI (Part-1 of text)2. mahishasuramardini (Part-2 of text)3. anasuyA (Part-3 of text)
The well known durgA saptashati is included in Part-2.
The presentation is in form of a dialogue between Shri parashurama and Shri dattatreya.The terminology is somewhat different from what many people here may be used to.There will also be sAmpradAya differences in view point - so take what you like, leave outwhat does not appeal to you. I personally have been able to reconcile everything in therewith veda-s, bhAgavatam, gitA, upanishad-s.
Hari om.
Regards,Shrinivas Gadkari
