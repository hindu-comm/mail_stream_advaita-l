KIRATA VARAHI STAVAH which is used for subjugation of one’s enemies (satru vashya and satru samhara). This stotra is used for counter attacking the abhicharika prayoga and is proved to be very effective as told by lotus feet.

One of the stanza says that : 
Haara nupura keyura katakai rupa shobhitaam Vairi patni kantasutra cchedinim krura rupinim

Wearing necklaces , anklets , crown and bangles her form shines, She has a cruel form which cuts the mangala suthra of the enemy’s wife.

The other stanza which is the need of the house goes like this:
Hanthu te musalah satrun aasaneh patana diva Satrugraamaan gruhaan desaan raashtraan pravisha sarvasah

Kill those enemies with a mace and upset their posture during the day, Destroy the villages of enemies , their houses, their states and country and Protect the Borders, Oh! Mother.
