Dear Members,
                                Today is Aswina Krishna Vidiya & Bhoumavasara-Aswini Nakshtra. It is indeed auspicious time to recite  Devi Upanishad . Reciting it today before the Mother Goddess will remove Apamrutyu bhayam, Please makes use of the day recite this upanishad and get the blessings of mother godess.
The Devi Upanishad comprises 32 verses after an invocation from the Atharvaveda. The text describes the goddess as the highest principle and the ultimate truth in the universe (Brahman).
It is based on Rigveda.
अहं राष्ट्री सङ्गमनी वसूनामहं सुवे पितरमस्य मूर्धन्मम योनिरप्स्वन्तः समुद्रे । य एवं वेद स

भौमाश्विन्यां महादेवी संनिधौ जप्त्वा महामृत्युं तरति
। य एवं वेदेत्युपनिषत् 

Sri Guru Padaravindarpana Mastu
