In this short file a few passages from the Shruti and Sutras along with
Bhashya are listed to show that the idea of 'sattaa traividhyam' is
admitted in the Upanishads and Sutra. The basis for this listing is a short
talk by the renowned scholar Sri Mani Dravid Sastrigal and another scholar
in Tamil.  This file, however, is in English with the quotations in
Sanskrit:
