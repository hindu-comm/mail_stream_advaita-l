अप्रत्याख्यायैव कार्यप्रपञ्चं परिणामप्रक्रियां चाश्रयति सगुणेषूपासनेषूपयोक्ष्यत इति
....................

shrI bhagavatpAda accepts परिणामप्रक्रिया for ease in सगुणोपासना.  This is the 
basis for introducing chandramoulIshwara puja & shrIyantra in AmnAya mutts
for welfare of mankind and also for people like us to follow the puja etc.
without throwing them into winds till the व्यवसायात्मिका बुद्धि is achieved.

rgs,
