 Dear friends, Such ideas as quoted by Sri Subramanian and Sri Sudhanshu Shekar 
are no longer valid in this century . 
They were ok for the period when they were made. 
Atma is not the essence of Brahmins only. 
Sruti says Atmata Eva idagaM sarvaM || Chandogya 7-26-1 
and atmaivEdagaM sarvam || Chandogya 7-25-2.Atman is the essence of all and everything.Whoever has the sincere longing for Atmajnana is 
an adhikari , irrespective of caste creed, religion or sex. 
They need not wear the ochre robe.To be an Atmajnani is the birth right of every human being.
With respectful namaskars,Sreenivasa Murthy

Yes, we have such clear statements. In Mundaka Bhashya 1.2.12: For the
mantra: परीक्ष्य लोकान्कर्मचितान्ब्राह्मणो निर्वेदमायान्नास्त्यकृतः कृतेन ।
where the word 'brahmana' occurs, Shankara says:

ब्राह्मणः, ब्राह्मणस्यैव विशेषतोऽधिकारः सर्वत्यागेन ब्रह्मविद्यायामिति
ब्राह्मणग्रहणम्

The mantra uses the word Brahmana since such a one alone has the adhikara
to renounce everything and engage in Brahmavidya.
