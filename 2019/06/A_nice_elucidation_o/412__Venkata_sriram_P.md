Namaste,

न रूपमस्येह तथोपलभ्यते नान्तो न चादिर्न च संप्रतिष्ठा।
अश्वत्थमेनं सुविरूढमूल मसङ्गशस्त्रेण दृढेन छित्त्वा।।15.3।।

The Gita says that this peepal tree called 'samsAra-vruksha' should be 
severed and uprooted with असङ्गशस्त्रः ie., vairAgya.  

Howsoever & whatsoever philosophies are discussed, unless this detachment is
dawn, the upAdhi-dosha is bound to remain.  

rgs,
sriram
