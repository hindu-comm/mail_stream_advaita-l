Namaste Subbuji
I presume you are drawing attention to the fact that in the gItA bhAShya
and mahAbhArata, the words avidyA and bhUta-prakRti / avyaktA are used
interchangeably at several places.

And hence it is implied that the idea that, in the wake of moxA, avidyA
alone goes away (abhAvagamanaM happens, as mentioned in bhAShya), while
mAyA continues, is not the position of bhAShya.

Right?

Om
Raghav
