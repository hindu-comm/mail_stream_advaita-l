How a Sannyasi should conduct  and the method of realizing the Advaitic
Supreme:

In the 29th chapter of the Second Part of this Purana is a long discourse
on the Sannyasin and his sadhana.  This Purana has been cited by Sri
Vishveshvara Saraswati (the Guru of Sri Madhusudana Saraswati) in the Yati
Dharma sangraha.

The entire chapter is the sadhana for the Sannyasin.  After stating the
prayaschitta for any error on the part of the Sannyasi, the sadhana for the
Advaita Atma Jnana praapti.

First the original shlokas are given and later the English translation is
given.
