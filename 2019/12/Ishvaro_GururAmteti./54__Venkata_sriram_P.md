Namaste,

पातञ्जलयोगसूत्राणि vide समाधि पादः (shloka no. 26) mentions :

स पूर्वेषाम् अपि गुरुः कालेनानवच्छेदात् ...

He (Ishwara) is the Teacher of all Ancient Teachers, being not limited
by Time.

So, Ishwara = Guru
