This is my understanding.  Please do correct me.

What is known as saguNa-brahman in vyaavahaarika is realized by j~naani
as nirguNa-brahman in paaramaarthika. In that sense, they are the same.

In the paaramaarthika satyam there is only nirguNa brahman and that
state is beyond duality.
