gabhiire kaasaare vishati vijane ghoravipine
vishaale shaile cha bhramati kusumaarthaM jaDamatiH |
samarpyaikaM chetaH sarasijamumaanaatha bhavate
sukhenaavasthaatuM jana iha na jaanaati kimaho || 9||
                                  shivAnandalaharI

The ignorant go in search of flowers to worship thee, O Lord of
umaa, by entering deep lakes, roaming in lonely and terrible
forests, and climbing huge mountains. It is indeed a wonder that
people do not know the truth that just by offering the lotus
flower of mind to thee, one can remain in the state of joy here.

In secret in my soul
I will gather blossoms for Thee
Dipping them in Devotion
I will lay them at Thy feet - Swami Yogananda
