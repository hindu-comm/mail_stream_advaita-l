naratvaM devatvaM nagavanamR^igatvaM mashakataa
pashutvaM kiiTatvaM bhavatu vihagatvaadi jananam.h |
sadaa tvatpaadaabjasmaraNaparamaanandalaharii\-
vihaaraasaktaM ched.hdhR^idayamiha kiM tena vapushhaa || 10||
                                       shivaanandalaharii


Approximate translation in English and then in Tamil:*
=======================================================


Does the nature of the body I assume matter my Lord when all my
thoughts flow towards thee and I am immersed in the waves of
bliss arising from contemplating on thy glory. Be that of a man,
or an animal roaming in forests and mountains, as a mosquito, or
any beast, or even a worm or a bird, the body I assume does not
matter, as long as my mind always contemplates on thee.
