Namaste All,


>1) Giving up the notion of "I am the doer" or sense of agency, kartrtva
>tyaagam. God alone is the doer. Having the notion, I am the doer is
>false. Hence we abandon this false notion.
>

This is interesting because I was not able to comprehend the full puport of
BG IV.14. I don't think I still understand it. If I remember correctly, this
is translated by Sri Warrier as "I (Sri Krishna) am the non-doer." The text
I was reading was BGB by Sri A.G. Krishnan Warrier.
