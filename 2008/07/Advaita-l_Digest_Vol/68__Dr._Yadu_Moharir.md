Namste:

As per devi bhaagavata there are 28 vyasa and kR^iShNadvaipaayana is mentioned as being the last one.

Thus vyaasaappears to baan upaadhii and not a vyaktinaama.

(1)brhmaa (2)prajaapatii (3) shukraacaarya (4) bR^ihaspatii (5) suurya (6) yama (7) indra (8) vasiShTa (9) saarasvata (10) tridhaamaa (11) trivR^iSha (12) bhaaradvaaja (13) antarixa (14) dharma (15) trayyaruNii (16) dha.njaya (17) medhaatithii (18) vratii (19) atrii 20) gautam (21) haryaatmaa 22) vaajashravaa 23) soma aamuShyaayaNa (24) tR^iNabi.nduu (25) bhaargava (26) shaktii (27) paraashara (28) kR^iShNadvaipaayana.
