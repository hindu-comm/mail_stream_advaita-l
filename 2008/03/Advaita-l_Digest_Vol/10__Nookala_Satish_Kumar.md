>  > vrIhyagra svavishANa ratnakalaSa prodyat karAmbhoruhaH
>  > dhyeyo vallabhayA sapadmakarayA SlishTojjvalad bhUshayA
>  > viSvotpatti vipatti samsthitikaro vighneSa ishTArthadaH :

>  > script).
