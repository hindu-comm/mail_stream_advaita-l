In the past we have discussed how there has been a long evolution in the 
meaning of the words samkhya and yoga.  In the earliest stages they simply 
meant "theory" and "practice" two approaches to analysis which were used 
in many different fields of knowledge (including Ayurveda as you note.) 
The dualistic philosophy known as "Capital-S" Samkhya is but one variety 
of "small-s" samkhya.
