Dear all,

  The book titled "Yoga, Enlightenment and Perfection" referred by Sh.Ramesh Krishnamurthy MahaSaya detailing the yogic experiences of Jagadguru Sh.Abhinava VidyA Tirtha MahasvaminaH has been translated into tamizh by Sh.Ramasubramainan mahaSaya with the tytle "Yogamum jnAnamum jIvanmukthiyum".  The book in tamizh has been published by Sri VidyatIrtha Foundation, Chennai.  

       While the quotations in the book are printed in Tamizh script, at the end of the book, under anubandam, all the quotations are again printed in Devanagari script.
