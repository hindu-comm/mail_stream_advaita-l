> "tisrEva dEvatA iti nairuktAH" - ie., the nirukta says that the main 
> vaidika devatAs are only 3 in number viz., agni, vAyu / indra, sUrya

> "agniH pruthivIsthAnO vAyurva indrO vA antarikSasthAnaH sUryO 
> dyusthAnaH" ie., the agni tattva presides over pruthivi; vAyu / indra

ChhAndogyopanishad 4.17.1:

prajApatirlokAnabhyatapatteShAM tapyamAnAnAM rasAnprAvR^ihadagniM 
pR^ithivyA vAyumantarikShAdAdityaN divaH ||

"Prajapati performed the tapa of meditating on the worlds and from them 
that were meditated on he squeezed out the essence; Agni from the earth, 
Vayu from the antariksha, and Aditya from heaven."
