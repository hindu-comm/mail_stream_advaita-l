> > Good day everyone.  As a relatively new student of Advaita Vedanta, I

> > been chanting and intoning Om throughout the day.

> > So I'm really curious:
> >
> > What have been your personal experiences chanting Om?

>
> Reciting Om or other mantras is not a Vedantic practice per se but a Yogic
> one though of course the two are rather intertwined.  Vedantic practice
> revolves around understanding the difference between what is real and
> unreal, separating oneself from the unreal, and acquiring knowledge of the
> real.  The other spiritual practices are a preparation for this. (By
> which I do not mean to imply that theey are not important in their own
> sphere.)
