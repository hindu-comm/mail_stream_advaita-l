Ved.Brahmasri Nochur Venkataraman will be discoursing on the 11th skandha
of the srimadbhAgavatam which contains explicit Advaitic teachings.  The
venue will be the Sringeri Shankara maTha, Bangalore.  The talk, in Tamil,
will be for a week from 3rd  to 9th March 2014.  There will be class on Sri
Shankaracharya's work 'Dashashloki' in the mornings.  The complete details
are available in the invitation uploaded here:

http://www.mediafire.com/view/1w7erwee57wwk71/Divya_Satsang.pdf
