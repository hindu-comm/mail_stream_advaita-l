> I am interested to know what was the outcome of debate between SSS and
> Sringeri acharyas.

There was never a formal debate of this kind. But there were debates between
groups of pundits espousing the two stances, "this is all bhAshya-viruddha" vs.
"this is only due to prakriyA-bheda" - notably an extensive debate in the 1960s.
