Namaste,

It is the 1st sloka itself.

IshwarAnugrahAdEva pumsAm advaita vAsanA
mahadbhayaparitrANA viprANAmupajAyatE ..............1

It is with the grace of Ishwara that one gets the advaita-vAsana that protects (saves)
the wise from the great fear called samsAra.

regs,
sriram
