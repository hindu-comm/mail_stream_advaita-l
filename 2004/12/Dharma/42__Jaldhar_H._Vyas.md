On Wed, 8 Dec 2004, Kiran B.R. wrote:

> In this regard, those who think it's strange if a saMnyAsI does karma
> must call to mind vyAsa who begot Pandu, Dhrutharashtra and Vidura.
> Was it action or what? Was it karma or what?

Yes it was.  As Aniruddhan said, Krshna Dvaipayana aka Veda Vyasa was a
grhastha.  His son Shukadeva was completely free of karma (not even having
yajnopavita, the sine qua non of karma for a Brahmana) and the Bhagavata
Purana calls him superior to his father because of it.
