Bharathiya Vidya Bhavan, Bombay published in 1980
*Victory over Manda* *Misra *page *161*

Sankara said to Mandana: "If I win you should take sannyasa, if you win I
shall become a house-holder *after performing the appropriate expiatory
ceremony or I will continue to perform expiation until death*"
