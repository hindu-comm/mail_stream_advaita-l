It is well known that there is this famous verse in the Mahabharata:

रुद्रो नारायणश्चश्चैव  *सत्त्वमेकं द्विधा कृतम्*।
लोके चरति कौन्तेय व्यक्तिस्थं सर्वकर्मसु।। 12-350-27a 12-350-27b.

[Rudra and Narayana are only two manifestations of One Principle.......]

We find the above verse paraphrased in the Padmapurana:

https://sa.wikisource.org/s/xot

विष्णुः शिवः शिवो* विष्णुरेकमूर्तिर्द्विधा स्थिता* |
तस्मात्सर्वप्रकारेण नैव निंदां प्रकारयेत्  ||

[Vishnu is Shiva and Shiva is Vishnu, one Form alone appears as two.  Hence
one aught not to denigrate either by any means.]
