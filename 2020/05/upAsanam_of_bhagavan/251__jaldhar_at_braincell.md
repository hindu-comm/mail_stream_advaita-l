kiMcha nAnAkarmabhistattaddevatAprItinimittAnyapi phalAni hariprItyA

mUlasekaM vinA tAH svasvaniShechanena | prANasyopahAro bhojanaM 
tasmAdevendriyANAM tR^iptiH natu tattadindriyeShu 
pR^ithakpR^ithagannalepanena | tathAchyutArAdhanameva sarvadevatArAdhanaM 
na pR^ithagityarthaH ||14||
