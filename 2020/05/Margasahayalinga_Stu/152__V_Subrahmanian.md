Margasahayalinga Stuti of Srimad Appayya DikshitaMargasahayalinga Stuti of
Srimad Appayya Dikshita

In this short work Dikshitendra sings in praise of Lord Margabandhu of
Tiruvirinchipuram, His Kuladevata. This Stotram was published in the 1933
edition of Sri KV Subrahmanya Sastrigal's Srimad Appayya Dikshitendra
Vijayam. The author mentions that this is an extract from a work of
Dikshitendra named Margabandhu-champu which is not available now.
