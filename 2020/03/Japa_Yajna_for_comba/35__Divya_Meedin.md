श्री गुरुभ्यो नमः

As per the directive of Jagadguru Sri Mahasannidhanam and Jagadguru Sri
Sannidhanam, all Guru bandhus are requested to undertake the attached
Sankalpa and chant the Durga Parameshwari Stotram as many times as possible
for the welfare of entire humanity in the wake of the coronavirus pandemic.

An audio clip and text in various scripts can be found here:

https://www.sringeri.net/2020/03/22/important-announcements/important-announcement-durga-parameshwari-stotram-with-sankalpa.htm


श्री गुरुभ्यो नमः

Regards,
Divya
