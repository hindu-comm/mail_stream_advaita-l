A very inspiring and enlightening short video talk by Sri Sarma Sastrigal.
Theme: A resolve by fathers of girls: 'I shall resolve to give my daughter
in marriage to a Vedic scholar.'

”வைதிகருக்கு என் பெண்ணை கல்யாணம் செய்து தர நான் சங்கல்பம் செய்துள்ளேன்.
பெண் சம்மதித்தால் நடைபெறும்”

If we are able to get speakers in Kannada, Telugu, etc. to talk on this
theme, it would go a long way in inspiring people to make the dream of
'propagating veda vidya and enabling the veda vidwans to lead a vedic
wedded life by getting a suitable partner.' Fathers of girls and girls
themselves have to rise up to this ideal: marrying someone in the Vaidika
Vrutti. I came across a BE girl marrying an archaka in the Srivaishnava
community. There are such rare gems of examples in other communities too.

Please share this message widely.

Om Tat Sat
