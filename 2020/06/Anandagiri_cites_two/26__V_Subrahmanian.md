In Anandagiri's gloss for Shankara's Ishavasya Bhashya, these two passages
are cited. The source of these could not be located despite a search.  The
purport of the passages is very profound and constitute a major pramana for
Advaita.

The second passage resembles the famous Mandukya Karika न निरोधो न
चोत्पत्तिः....

'ब्रह्मैव सर्वमात्मैव सत्प्रकाशाविशेषतः । हेयोपादेयभावोऽयं न
सन्स्वप्नवदीर्यते ॥'
उक्तं च - `न बन्धोऽस्ति न मोक्षोऽस्ति न विकल्पोऽस्ति तत्त्वतः । नित्यप्रकाश
एवास्ति विश्वाकारो महेश्वरः ॥'

Anyone may try locating the source.

Thanks.
