#Vivekachudamani Part 17
The seventeeth talk, consists of the commentary to verse 10 of the
vivekachUDAmaNi by Jagadguru Srimad Chandrashekhara Bharati Mahaswamigal.
https://youtu.be/vpKHZBuzDIA संन्यस्य सर्वकर्माणि भवबन्धविमुक्तये । यत्यतां
पण्डितैर्धीरैरात्माभ्यास उपस्थितैः ॥ १० ॥ Here AchArya considers the charge
made by the pUrva-mImAmsaka that renouncing all action as part of jnAna
sAdhana goes against the injunctions of the shruti. shruti-s mantra
referred to in the talk: 1) कुर्वन्नेवेह कर्माणि जिजीविषेच्छतं समाः । एवं
त्वयि नान्यथेतोऽस्ति न कर्म लिप्यते नरे ॥ २ ॥ (ISAvAsya upaniShad mantra 2)
