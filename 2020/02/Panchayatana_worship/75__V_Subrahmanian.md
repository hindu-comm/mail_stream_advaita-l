Thanks Sriram ji for the verse which is in this chapter
https://sa.wikisource.org/s/hst   It is about Shakti panchayatana where the
other four will be in the four corners.

भवानीं तु यजेन्मध्ये तथैशान्यां तु माधवम् ।
आग्नेय्यां गिरिजानाथं गणेशं रक्षसां दिशि ॥ ३७ ॥
वायव्यामर्चयेत्सूर्यमिति देवस्थितिक्रमः ।

The entire discourse is by Narayana.
