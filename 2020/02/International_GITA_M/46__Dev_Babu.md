*Harih Om*

*As some of you are already aware, Sydney people will be blessed to
witness one of the largest events of recent times in March 2020.  After
successful international events in Mauritius and UK in 2019, the
Kurukshetra Development Board sponsored by Haryana Government, Bharat are
organizing International Gita Mahotsav  (IGM) in Sydney from 21st March to
24th March 2020.  Briefly, the event includes the following major
activities during the 4 day period:*

*·        International Gita Seminar – Sat 21st March at Darling Harbour
Theatre, ICC Sydney*

*·        Gita Yagna – Sunday 22nd March at Sri Mandir, Auburn*

*·        Gita Sadbhavana Yatra – Sun 22nd March afternoon.*

*·        Gita Mahaa Aarti and Cultural Performances - Sun 22nd March
evening*

*·        Reception in Federal Parliament, Canberra – Mon 23rd March*

*·        Valedictory function at NSW Parliament – Tue 24th March.*

*Full details of all the above activities are given in the attached flyers.*

*कर्मण्येवाधिकारस्ते  मा फलेषु कदाचन।  मा कर्मफलहेतुर्भूर्मा ते
सङ्गोत्स्त्वकर्मणि॥ (2-47)*

*karmaNyevaadhikaaraste  maa phaleShu kadaacana |   maa
karmaphalaheturbhuurmaa te sangotstvakarmaNi ||*

*You have freedom in (performing) action and never in the results
thereof.  May you not become the author of the results of action, nor may
you have attachment in inaction. *
