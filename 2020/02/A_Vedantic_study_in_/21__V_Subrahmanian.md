The chapter consists of a stuti of Rudra, the Para Brahman, by Brahma, the
chaturmukha and the blessing of the former in reply to the stuti.


The Sankskrit version is here in transliterated form:


http://www.sanskrit-linguistics.org/dcs/index.php?contents=texte&PhraseID=442626

The English translation is available here:

https://docs.google.com/document/d/1taSFyCqeKPzvG5_mvjhA27WyXc7uthSC8kUeTTBErAQ/edit?usp=sharing

The reading of the chapter takes us thru a journey of Vedanta touching upon
the ideas of creation, the nature of Para Brahman, both saguna and Nirguna.
