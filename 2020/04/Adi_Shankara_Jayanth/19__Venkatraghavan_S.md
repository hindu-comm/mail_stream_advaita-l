*Shankara Jayanthi Programme*

Friday, April 24 - Tuesday April 28, 2020


With the benign blessings of their Holinesses, Sri Sri Mahasannidhanam &
Sri Sri Sannidhanam, Shankara Jayanthi (Tuesday, 28 April 2020) is being
marked with a special 5 day online mahotsavam starting from Friday, 24
April to Tuesday, 28 April 2020.


A short video flyer of the proposed programme can be found here:
https://youtu.be/goh1PMV8bcI

• Ekadasha Vara Rudra Parayanam: 7 AM - 11 AM IST


• Adi Shankara Ashtotara Namavali Parayanam:  4 PM - 5:30 PM IST
