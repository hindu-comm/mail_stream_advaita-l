In the 'Ekachakram' rUpakam of MM Sri Ranganatha Sharma,
https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=KlNpiVQ9hPE&feature=youtu.be there is a
dialogue that is interesting in that it reminds us of Shankara
Bhagavatpada's words in the Taittiriya Bhashyam: After 32 minutes in the
video, Bakasura says: इदमस्ति मे भागदेयम्.....for which Bhimasena replies:
इदं  मे स्वस्त्ययनम् - यन्मां.....          This reminds us of the Bhashya
vakya - एतदेव मे स्वस्त्ययनम्  यन्मामेकयोगिनमनेकयोगिबहुप्रतिपक्षमात्थ ।
