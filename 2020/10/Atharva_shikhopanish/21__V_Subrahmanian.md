  This statement about the Trimurtis, Indra, etc. being born of the

In the 11th verse below the very words of the Upanishad are present with
only a slight variation:
http://satsangdhara.net/shiva/shiva-01-03.htm   The Purana starts with the
declaration that the Vedanta purport is being stated and goes on to present
the Vedantic Brahman.  Shankara has cited the Shiva Puranam in the VSN

present in the verse 9 below, as 'the Cause of all causes':
