This is an announcement about an initiative from the Sringeri Peetham for
recording discourses on a variety of topics such as the Madhaviya Shankara
Vijaya, Shankara's stotra literature, Prakarana texts, Mahabharata Shanti
Parva, moral stories in the Upanishads, etc.. and upload them every second
and fourth Saturday of the month. These will be available on the Mutt
website for all to access any time. HH Sri  Mahasannidhanam will deliver a
discourse inaugurating the project and HH Sri Sannidhanam will declare open
the dedicated website on 26th October 2020.
