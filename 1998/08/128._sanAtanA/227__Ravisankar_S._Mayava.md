128. sanAtanA

SHE who is ancient. Her form is eternal and perfect. In this
context refer to "ajonityaH shAsvatoyaM purANaH" [kaThopaniShad]
- which means " He is unborn, eternal, ever lasting and ancient"

AUM sanAtanAyai namaH
