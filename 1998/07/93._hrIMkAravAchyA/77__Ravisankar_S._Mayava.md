93. hrIMkAravAchyA

SHE is expressed by the word hrIM. Only when the brahman is limited by
mAyA, the possibility of expressing its attribute arises. SHE is the
conventional meaning of hrIM.

AUM hrIMkAravAchyAyai namaH
