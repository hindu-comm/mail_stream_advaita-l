85. hrI.nkAramantrA

SHE is the mantra hrIM. SHE save those who meditate on hrIM, because SHE
is the mantra (panchadashi) in which hrIM is set or because SHE is one
with the hrIM which expresses HER.

AUM hrI.nkAramantrAyai namaH
