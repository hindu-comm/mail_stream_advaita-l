99. hrIm.h

SHE who is hrIm.h.

hrIm.h is derived from the root hR^i which means to take away. While SHE
is said to be the basis for every attribute like art, wealth, power, etc.
SHE confers ultimate liberation by taking them all away.

AUM hrIm.h namaH
