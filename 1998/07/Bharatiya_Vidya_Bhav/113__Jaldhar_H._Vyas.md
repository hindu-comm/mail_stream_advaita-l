Ignorance can only be removed by the ignorant person himself.  All that
one can hope to do is to provide more opportunities for people to remove
their ignorance.

We know from the shastras that Truth alone wins out.  Indeed a look
at the history of all the phony modern sects that have sprung up reveals
that few last more than a century--if that.  While our Dharma is still
going strong after millenia.
