If we fix our mind in Truth, we can do anything which naturally comes to
us.


>I have two alternatives.
>
>1. Ignore the world, forget responsibilities and GO
>2. Stay and discharge responsibilities.

Since "Brahmam only Exists", we need not ignore anything. Just stay in
Brahmam and do the duties.
