167. harit pati samArAdhyA

The word "harit" means cardinal points in space and "pati" means
Lord. This term indicates mahendra and others who are the lords
of thse cardinal points. SHE is fit to be worshipped by them with
devotion and faith. SHE helps them by destroying their enemies
and SHE is the deity who grants their wishes.

AUM haritpatisamArAdhyAyai namaH
======================
>From  a translation of shankara bhAshyam of shrI lalita trishatI
