147. kaThinasthanamaNDalA

SHE whose breasts are hard and firm. sthana maNDala indicates the
regions are situated from the beginning to end. These are hard,
that is, very firm. SHE has such breasts.

AUM kaThinasthanamaNDalAyai namaH
