> There are 2 versions of Bhagavan Ramana's ashottara-shatanAmAvaLi.  One
> composed by Sri.Vishwanatha Swami
> another by Sri.Jagadeesa Sastrigal.
>
> In the version of Sri.Jagadeesha Sastrigal, the 8th name is :
>
> श्रृंगाद्रिमठाधीशभाविताय नमो नमः
>
> Upon asking Bhagaan by a devotee, Bhagavan replied it points towards Sri
> Satchidananda Sivabhinava Nrisimha
> Bharati Mahaswamigal of Sringeri. His Holiness had high regards for
> Bhagavan and whoever used to visit
> Sringeri from Tiruvannamalai, used to enquire about the health of Bhagavan.
>
> Also, couple of highly acclaimed Acharya from Puri Govardhan Mutt
> approached Bhagavan for a clarification on certain shloka
> which runs as "hara-gaurI samyOgAt....".  Bhagavan glanced at Puri
> Acharya.  They exchanged the glances and it
> was perfect Silence.  Later, Puri Acharya left the placed satisfied with
> the answer !
>
> Sringeri Sivaganga Acharya (Sri.Subramanya Bharati Swamigal) used to seek
> guidance from Bhagavan.
>
> So, these are some of the examples that show that traditional acharyas
> held Bhagavan in high esteem.
