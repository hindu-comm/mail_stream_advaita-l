> 'Jnanam alone is; there is no Jnani'

praNAms 
Hare Krishna

But for  all practical purposes we do identify the edifice of jnAnaM in the BMI of jnAni and attribute avidyA lesha, prArabda karma to this jnAni and holding this complex as true we do differentiate between  videha mukti, Jeevan mukti/sadyO mukti etc. And yes, when we realize that when 'jnAnaM' alone is, there will not be any dvandva or dvaita like jnAni, ajnAni, Atma, anAtma, jeeva, Ishwara, jagat etc. 

Hari Hari Hari Bol!!!
bhaskar
