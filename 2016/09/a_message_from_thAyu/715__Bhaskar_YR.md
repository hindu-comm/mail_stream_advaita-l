Would like to know how 'summa-iru' and nAn yaar (who am I) are traditionally accepted method to do brahma jignAsa.

Yoga Vashishta takes off with this enquiry "Who am I" .  Bhagavan Ramachandra poses this question as:

किं मे राज्येन किं भोगैः "कोऽहं" किमदमागतम् । यन्मिथ्यैवास्तु तन्मिथ्या कस्य नाम किमागतम् ॥
