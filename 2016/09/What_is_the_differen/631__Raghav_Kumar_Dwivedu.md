A correction in the fourth pada of the verse from panchdashI -

IkShaNAdi praveshAntA sRShTiH Ishena kalpitA

jAgradAdi vimokShAntaH samsAro jivakalpitaH

It was wrongly given as sRShTiH jivakalpitaH instead of samsAro
jIvakalpitaH.

This shloka is also there in one of the minor upanishads called the
varAhopaniShad.

Om
