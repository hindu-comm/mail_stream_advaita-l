'Gūḍhārtha Dīpikā' - Kannada Translation Released

Namaste

 Sri Madhusudana Saraswati's commentary on the Bhagavadgītā, 'Gūḍhārtha
Dīpikā', has been translated to Kannada and published by Vedanta Bharati
recently.  Prof.M.A.Hegde, Siddapura, has done the translation. The book,
in A4 size, Hardbound, in over 600 pages, is priced at Rs.450. Available
with:

Sri Nagaraj, Ramashankaraprasada, 22nd Main, BSK 2nd stage (opp. Aurobindo
school)  Phone: 9449912121

Sringeri Shankara Maṭha, Book Stall, Bangalore 560004.
