Namaste Praveenji,

Thank you for the information. The book, I referred to, appears to be the thesis of Dr. Shastry submitted to the Karnatak university in 1976. The aim of the thesis, as stated,  was to review the history of the Sringeri math from the 14th century CE to 19th century CE and for that purpose the thesis is a contribution.

As regards the Guruparampara, he states that from 820 (CE) to the commencement of Shri Vidya Sankara Tirtha's accesion, the dates cannot be fixed. However he has given the date of accesion of Shri Bharati Krishna Tirtha and not the date of accesion of  Shri Vidyashankara Tirtha. The thesis just gives the date of Adi Shankaracharya as 820 CE and is not concerned with the  disclosure of Shri Satchidananda Shiva Abhinava Narasimha Bharati, that Adi Shankara was born on the 14th year of the reign of Vikramaditya. 

Regards,
Sunil K,B,
