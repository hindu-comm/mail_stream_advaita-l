The relevant part of the verse reads so in Sanskrit: न च साऽद्य पुनर्भूयः
स्मृतिर्मे सम्भविष्यति । The explanation may not have been given, but the
translation is not incorrect, since it literally reads as: "And now, that
recollection of mine [regarding the narration] is not possible".
