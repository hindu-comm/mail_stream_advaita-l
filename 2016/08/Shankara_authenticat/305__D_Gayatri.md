> This jAbala Upanishad can viewed as a stuti, to impel one to chant the
> Rudram but it cannot be dismissed as a guNavAda, a falsehood.


Shri Venkatraghavan, That is what I intended to imply when I used the
word arthavAda. However, please note that this does not imply Shiva is
considered as brahman here in any way because all devatas are fit to
be worshipped and Shiva is one of them.
