> Whoever has read the Uttarakhanda of the Ramayana  (Ramayana Vedasamam)

> is how the Varna pratha came into being. I read it years ago and never felt
> the need of revising that. That is what has also been ratified by Lord
> Krishna in the Bhagavad Gita. This is the essence of the Varna pratha for
> all time. Any other treatment on the Varna pratha is simply the matter of
> details.
