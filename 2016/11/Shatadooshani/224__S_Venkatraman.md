I am reminded of what Ramakrishna Paramahamsa told a man counting mangoes in an orchard, 'Why are you wasting your time counting the mangoes when it can be more usefully spent eating and enjoying them?'

Ramana goes a step further and says 'சும்மா இரு (Just be)'. 

But we can only be ourselves.
