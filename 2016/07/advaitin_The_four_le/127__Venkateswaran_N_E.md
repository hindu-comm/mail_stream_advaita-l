As per srImad bhAgavataM they are :
tapaH—austerity, 
sauchaM—cleanliness, 
daya—mercy, 
satyaM —truthfulness

As per varAha purANa they are :
guNa-quality
dravya-substance
kriya-action
jAti-genus

Warm Regards
Venkateswaran N E
