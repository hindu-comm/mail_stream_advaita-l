> Hindu tradition is to give due respect to deserving people irrespective of
> their religion or nationality.
> This attested and made evident by by Varahamihira in Brihat Samhita 2-14.
>
> म्लेच्छाहि यवनास्तेषु सम्यक् शास्त्रं प्रतिष्ठितम् ।
> ऋषिवत्तेऽपि पूज्यन्ते किं पुनर्दैवविद्विजः।।
>
> Because of their entrenchment in the Khagola Sastra Yavanas though Mlecchas
> are adored as Rishis. What to say if one is a brahmin
> astronomer/astrologer.


Yes they can be respected but this Sloka is not about situation when the
Mlecchas are writing Sastras contradicting Brahmin Sastras. If a Mleccha is
contradicting Adi Sankara and making wrong comments on Acharyas of
Parampara in any Sastra he will be not respected by us.
