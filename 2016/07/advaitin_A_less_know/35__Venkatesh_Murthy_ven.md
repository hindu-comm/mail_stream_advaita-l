Namaste

But we cannot neglect Appayya Diksita's words and other Srutis and
Smrutis asking Brahmins to worship Siva. As a matter of fact Siva is
the foremost God for Brahmins. Vishnu is partial to Kshatriyas. In
many Avatars Vishnu is Kshatriya only. Therefore for Brahmins Siva is
the foremost God. Without worshiping Siva if a Brahmin is worshipping
others he is ruined. For Vaishyas Brahma is the God and for Sudras
Indra or Ganapati are gods.
त्वं देवानां ब्राह्मणानामधिपतिर्विष्णुः क्षत्रियाणामधिपतिः ।
ब्राह्मणानां ब्राह्मणैराश्रीयते इत्यादि श्रुतिषु ब्राह्मणानां
शिवोपासनस्यैव विधेयत्वात् ।

ब्राह्मणानां शिवो देवः क्षत्रियाणान्तु माधवः । वैश्यानान्तु तथा
ब्रह्मा शूद्राणां सुरनायकः ।

ब्राह्मणो भगवान् रुद्रः क्षत्रियः परमो हरिः । पितामहस्तथा वैश्यो
वृषलस्तु पुरन्दरः ॥ इत्यादि मनुपाराशर्यादिषु वैदिकब्राह्मणानां
शिवदेवत्वश्रवणात्।

> Much before Appayya Dikshitar, Veda Vyasa himself has said in the
> Mahabharata:
>
> रुद्रो नारायणश्चैव सत्त्वमेकं द्विधा कृतम्।
> लोके चरति कौन्तेय व्यक्तिस्थं सर्वकर्मसु।। 12-350-27a 12-350-27b.
>
> It is One Principle that appears in the world as Rudra and Narayana.
>
> No one can say that it is an interpolation by a later Advaitin for even the
> 11th CE Andhra Bharatamu has annotated this verse along with a number of
> other Hari-Hara abheda verses from the original MB.
