> The answer is a very definite NO. The pot/clay or ornament/gold
> illustration applies ONLY to the modifications in sama satta (समसत्ता)
> context for cause/effect (कारण कार्य). It is not applicable in vishama
> satta(विषमसत्ता) context for cause/effect. The illustrations are
> applicable for all worldly modifications, all the way from mAyA to any
> other vyavaharika vastu. They are not applicable directly for world( any
> vastu)/Brahman relationship. But it is permissible to apply the vivarta
> illustration for Brahman/any other vastu at vyavaharika level (say gold or
> clay) and apply the pariNAma illustration for subsequent modifications of
> gold/clay ( say ornament/pot)  to arrive at the ultimate vivarta
> relationship between Brahman/ornament or pot directly.
