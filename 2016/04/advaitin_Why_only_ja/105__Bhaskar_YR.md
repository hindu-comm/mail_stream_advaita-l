ananyatvepi kAryakAraNayOH kAryasya kAraNAtmatvaM NA TU KAARANASYA KAARYAATMATVAM.  The ornament in its nAma & rUpa gold only but in gold there is no vyavahAra / vikAra of nAma rUpa.

praNAms Sri Aurobindo prabhuji
Hare Krishna

Apt observation.  Interestingly shankara clarifies ' this jagat what we perceive is NOT like mithyA sarpa on the rajju  and clarifies that this jagat is nothing but 'vishesha' darshana of that kAraNa and there is no mithyA whatsoever from that kAraNa drushti.
