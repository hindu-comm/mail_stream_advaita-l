Two hitherto unpublished Sanskrit works, now published


The Mannargudi Periyava Seva Samithi, Chennai has brought out two hitherto
unpublished works in the last two years:

1. Shiva Mahimakalikā stuti of Appayya Dikshita with a commentary of
Mannargudi MM Sri Tyagaraja Sastrigal (1815-1903) and a translation thereof
in English and Tamil by Vidwan Sri Goda Venkateswara Sastri. Price Rs.100.

2. Shikhariṇī Mālā of Appayya Dikshita with his own commentary Shiva Tattva
Viveka and a commentary thereon 'shiva tattva viveka dipika' by the above
Sri Tyagaraja Sastrigal edited with footnotes by Vidwan Sri Mani Dravid
Sastri. Price Rs.100.

They can be had, on personal request,  from the Trustee of the Mannargudi
Samithi, Chennai.
