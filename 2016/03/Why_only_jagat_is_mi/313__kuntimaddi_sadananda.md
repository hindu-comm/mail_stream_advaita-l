On Sat, 3/19/16, Anand Hudli via Advaita-l <advaita-l at lists.advaita-vedanta.org> wrote:

  brahma satyaM jaganmithyA jIvo brahmaiva nAparaH |
  jIvanmuktastu tadvidvAn.h iti
 vedAnta-DiNDimaH||

The alternate version of the second line is 

anEna vEdyam tat shAstram iti Vedanta dindimaH|

that by which the brahma satyam, jagan mithyaa and jivo brahmaiva nAparaH - is known, is the real science or science of reality while all other sciences are only science of the false!

Hari Om!
Sadananda
