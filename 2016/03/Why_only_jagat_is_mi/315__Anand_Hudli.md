Dear Shri Sadanandaji,

jIvanmuktastu tadvidvAn.h iti  vedAnta-DiNDimaH

>anEna vEdyam tat shAstram iti Vedanta dindimaH|

jIvanmuktastu tadvidvAn.h iti  vedAnta-DiNDimaH

Both are good descriptions, although the first one captures one of the
special features of advaita, namely the concept of "jIvanmukta". There is
no such concept in other schools of vedAnta, as far as I know.

Anand
