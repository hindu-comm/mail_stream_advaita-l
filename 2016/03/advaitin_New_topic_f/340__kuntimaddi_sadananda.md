       Sadaji, pranAms..
 I can share from my my own personal
 experience..
 Though I was born in a family and also got
 married to a family, where religious traditions were
 followed to a large extent, I was not a highly religious
 person (of there is such a term). I used to visit temples
 almost on a daily basis , believed in God and has a lot of
 questions (to which I sometimes got answers but also did not
 at other times).. My gravitation towers vedAntA a little
 more than half a decade back, made me to understand Hinduism
 as a religion better.. To me hence, one does not need to be
 religious to become spiritual.. But when one becomes
 spiritual, one understands religion better :-)
