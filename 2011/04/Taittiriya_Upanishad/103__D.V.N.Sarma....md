One can see the freedom of jnani in the leelas of Lord Dattatreya.
Giving darsan to a devotee drinking liquor and embracing a women etc.
Interpretations apart, the main intent is to shatter the set mental moulds
and
behavior patterns of the devotee. Flushing away the accumulated debris
from the mind.
