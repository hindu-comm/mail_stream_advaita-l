I am a student of Advaita Vedanta. I was privileged to study Advaita 
Vedanta and other necessary Shashtras in a traditional Gurukula System for 
a period of Six years. I have also undergone training in Yajurveda and 
have completed samhita of Yajurveda. I have also undergone training in 
Yoga sutras (Vyasa Bhashya). All my studies were in the medium of 
Sanskrit.  I have now taken up PhD from university of Mysore, trying to 
study the Tri-guna concept from a psychological perspective. Also working 
in a reputed college in Bangalore, teaching soft skills and Indian 
Psychology.
