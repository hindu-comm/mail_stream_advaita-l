chidAbhAsa = chit + AbhAsa,

where chit = shuddha chaitanya
AbhAsa = reflection

Essentially, chidAbhAsa is the reflected chit, i.e. chit conditioned by the
antaHkaraNa (the upAdhi corresponding to the individual).
