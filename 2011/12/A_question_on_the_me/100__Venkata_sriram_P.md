Namaste,

Pu.Mim. generally deals with yAgAs & kratus and and domain of dharma
as far as Pu.Mim. is concerned is restricted to thedefinition given below:

yAga is defined as "dEvata uddEshEna dravya tyAgaH yAgaH" and
dharma is defined as "yAgAdirEva karmah dharmah". 

regs,
