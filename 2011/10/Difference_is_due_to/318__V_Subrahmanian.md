I was not aware of the above mantra.  However there is a very similar
sounding mantra in the Brihadaranyaka Upanishad ii.v.19.  These two mantras
are of the same 'category' that Shankara has cited in that passage: One
Reality alone appears owing to upAdhis as many.  Gaudapada too has cited
this mantra in the kArika:
*नेह नानेति* चाम्नायादिन्द्रो मायाभिरित्यपि ।
अजायमानो बहुधा मायया जायते तु सः । ।माण्डूक्योपनिषत्कारिका _३.२४
