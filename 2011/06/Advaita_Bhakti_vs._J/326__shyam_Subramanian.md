Pranams. Your title I humbly submit is fallacious. Bhakti without Jnana is incomplete. Jnana without Bhakti is impossible. At their culmination the two are homologous. 
You may find this perspective - http://www.adi-shankara.org/2007/03/does-devotion-or-bhakti-have-role-in.html - to be of interest.
