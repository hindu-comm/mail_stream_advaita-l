Hare Krishna. I dont know why you say that the internal inconsistency is
an allegation. There are two primary classifications


   1. aneka iva vada (many jivas)
   2. eka jiva vada (one jiva)

aneka jiva vada is classified in to

   1. abhasa vada (semblence) - vartika
   2. pratibimba vada (reflection)
   3. avacheda vada (delimitation)

abhasa vada (vartika) has two types


   1. isvara and saksi are one
   2. isvara and saksi are differnt

 pratibimbha vada has two types


   1. isvara (maya upahita caitanya) reflected on mind avidya conditioned by
   mind is jiva (vivarana)
   2. bimba (maya upahita caitanya) reflected on avidya is is isvara,
   bimba reflected on antahkarana is jiva (samkshepa sariraka)
