> Dear Mahadevan Ji,

> A couple of years back, i had a similar doubt but when asked Shri Bharati Tirtha Swamigal, His Holiness clarified the sankhya-traya as:
>  
> 1) ni-rIshwara sAnkhya
> 2) sa-Ishwara sAnkhya
> 3) sAnkhya (jnAna) yOga (vide jnAna yOgEna sAnkhya buddhiH...)
>  
> Also there is another birudAvaLi that comes in the 1st line itself ie., pada vAkya pramANa.
>  
> His Holiness clarified this as:
>  
> pada = vyAkaraNa
> vAkhya = mimAmsa
> pramANa = nyAya
>  
> Hope it is clear now.
