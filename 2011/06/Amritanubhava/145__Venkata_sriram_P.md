Chowkhamba Publishers has printed this work and their respective outlets
across the country do have this. 

It has the original marathi verse in "ovi" chandas with marathi commentary
and hindi translation. Prof. R.Dandekar Ji (Head of the Dept. of 
Philosophy in Puna University) had translated from Marathi to Hindi.
Prof. R.Dandekarji, holds Doctoral Degree in Philosphy whose thesis
work was on Sant Jnaneshwar's great work & commentary on 
Bhagavat Gita called Jnaneshwari.Dandekarji'sPh.D guide
was Mahamahopadhyaya Mimamsakesari Acharya V.K.Dongre Ji.
