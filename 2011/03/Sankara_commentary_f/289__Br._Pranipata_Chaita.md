Hari Om! Pranaams!

For a change, let us keep aside what shankara says and consider what shuka says about courting, dancing etc., 'for a thing which is available freely in this world for all creatures right from a worm in ditch, why should one want mokSha for enjoying this and long for?'

In Shri Guru Smriti,
Br. Pranipata Chaitanya
