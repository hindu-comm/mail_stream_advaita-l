On Bheeshma Ashtami day all are required to give Tarpana and Arghya to
Bheeshma Pitamaha because  he died without any children.  If a person
does Sraddha of Bhishma he will get children.

Tarpana Mantra with Apasavya -

Vaiyaghrapadmagotraya SankRtyapravarayaCha Ganga putraya Bheeshmaya
Ajanmabrahmacharine
AputrayaJalam DadmiNamo Bhishmaya VarmineBhishmaha Santanavo
Veeraha Satyavadee Jitendriyaha
AbhiradbhiravapnotuPutrapautrochitam Kriyam

Arghya Mantra with Savya -

Vasunam Avataraya Santanoratmajaya Cha Arghyam Dadami Bheeshmaya
Abalyabrahmacharine

Regards

-Venkatesh
