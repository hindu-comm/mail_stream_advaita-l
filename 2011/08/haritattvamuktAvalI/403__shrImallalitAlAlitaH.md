A stuti of shrI-hari by shrI-sha~NkarAchArya is sung by many of us starting
as
*
स्तोष्ये भक्त्या विष्णुमनादिं जगदादिं*
*यस्मिन्नेतत्संसृतिचक्रं भ्रमतीत्थम् ।*
*यस्मिन्दृष्टे नश्यति तत्संसृतिचक्रं*
*तं संसारध्वान्तविनाशं हरिमीडे ॥*

- in matta-mayUra-chhanda.
You can find full version <http://bit.ly/raq0GB> at blog of shrI P R
Ramamurthy <http://bit.ly/qFRgPe>, called Gleanings of Sanskrit
Literature<http://bit.ly/rkBkoL>
.
It praises shrI-hari and like shrI-nR^isiMha-viGYApanam
<http://bit.ly/okJGEY>teaches advaita-vedAnta at the same time.
I'm editing and publishing it's commentary, called
haritattvamuktAvalI<http://bit.ly/oLGcib>,
by shrI-svayamprakAsha-yati. It is very helpful to reveal it's meaning.
