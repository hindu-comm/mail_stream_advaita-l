Dear sriram
It is true that the following slokah is from Ishavasyopanishath;

"Hiranmayena patrena sathyasyapihitam!
tattvam Pushan apvrunu sathyadhatmaya drstaye!!

But,it is not as what you have wriiten as below:

Dhiranmayena patrena sathyasapihitam mukham!
tattvam pushan apavrunu sathyadharmaya drstaye!!
N.Srikanta.
