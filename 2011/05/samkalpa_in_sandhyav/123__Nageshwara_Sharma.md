I am Telugu Smartha Brahmin (Veginati Vyedika sub-group). In our Sampradaya,
we say complete Sankalpam for Sandhyavandanam. So it starts with
PanchaShatha Koti Visthirna Mahi Madale.....It includes desha, kala kirtana.

Regards,
Nageshwara Sharma
