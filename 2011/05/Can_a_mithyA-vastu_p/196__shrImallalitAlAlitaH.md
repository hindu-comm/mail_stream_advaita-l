>
> Maricikajalajnane Api Tejaha
> Prithivyorapi Ambuno Vidyamanatvat Indriyadoshena Tejaha Prithivyoho
> Agrahanat Adrushtavashacca Ambunaha Grahanat Yatharthatvam. The mirage
> water is real water.
>

Its delusion as eyes see water in wrong quantity.
Moreover, tR^ivR^itkaraNam of pR^ithivi, etc. is said in upaniShad and not
dvivR^itkaraNam of silver and shell. So, there is no silver in shell. Hence,
seeing silver is a delusion.
