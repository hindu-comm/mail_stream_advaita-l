On this sacred day of SriNrsimha Jayanti I offer my humble Namaste to SriLakshmiNrsimha:

"Satyajnana sukha svarupa mamalam
kshirabdhi madhyesthitam, yogarudamapi
prasanna vadanam bhushasahasrojvalam
trakshamchakshu pinakasabhayavaran
bhibhrana markascavim ,chatribhuta Phanindra
mindudavalam SriLaksmiNrsimham Bhaje!!"

Namo LakshmiNrsimha!

N.Srikanta.
