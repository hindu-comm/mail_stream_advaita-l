A book titled 'Advaita siddhi' with the original and a Tamil rendering has
been brought out under the collective authorship of eminent Vidvans: 1. Sri

Dravid SastriNaH in 2008. The book covers some topics of the main Work.  It
has an English preface by Sri Goda SastriNaH. Price: Rs.150 and availability
with the Author.
