A program for devout pārāyaṇam of the 108 names addressed to Sri
Shankaracharya is initiated by the Jagadguru HH Sri Bharati Tirtha
Mahaswaminaḥ.  HH Sri Sannidhanam, Jagadguru Sri Sri Vidhushekhara Bharati
Mahaswaminaḥ has given a brief benedictory address in Kannada on the
greatness and need for the parayanam. The other details of the program are
available here:

http://www.sringeri.net/ashtottara-yajna

All Advaitins are welcome to involve themselves in this program in whatever
possible manner. Please also forward this message to all your friends and
other groups. The guidelines for the conduct of the program is
available.below.

warm regards
subrahmanian.v
Sri Shankaracharya Ashtottarla  Shatanaama Paarayana Yajna
