Pranams,

Pranava Sahasranama is utilised for making laksharchana at the jeevasamadhi
of Sri Sadasiva Brahmendra at Nerur.  The same was compiled by one Mr
Nagarajan.  During the aradhana festival of Sri Sadasiva Brahmendra, which
is normally taking place on Vaisaka Sukla Dasami, (5 days from Sri Sankara
Jayanthi) the laksharchana is being conducted.

Namaskarams,

G V RAMASWAMY
