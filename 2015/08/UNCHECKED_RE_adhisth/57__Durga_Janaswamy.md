> What is adhisthana devatA? 
>  
> The Vedanta shastra has specified certain deities as 'presiding'  
> deities for various organs, constituents of our body.  Thus we have: 
>  
> Who are adhisthana devatAs? 
>  
> Agni presides over vāk, sūrya for eye, etc.  The idea is, the blessing  
> of these deities is required for the proper functioning of these  
> organs.  When, owing to the karma of the jiva, there is the withdrawal  
> of the blessing of that devatā, that particular organ ceases to  
> function properly or totally.  These devatās are also called abhimāni  
> devatās. 
>  
> If one lives a good life adhering to the do's and don'ts of the  
> shāstram, there is no need to say that all the devatās will be pleased  
> and bless him.
