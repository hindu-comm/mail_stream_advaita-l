On Sat, 31 Jan 2015, Jaldhar H. Vyas via Advaita-l wrote:

> "A Brahmana should receive upanayana at 8 years.  Or eight from 
> conception.[1] [Then after giving the ages for Kshatriyas and Vaishyas] Or

One more thing I forgot to add.  Shastras also say that if one desires his 
son to have brahmavarchas he should give upanayana at five years old.


It should be noted that Swami Vidushekhar Bharati in his purvashrama 
received upanayana at that age.
