Bhagawan Sankaracharya says in Brahma Sutra 3.2.21 दर्शनाच्च (उभयलिङ्गाधिकरणम्) that
ब्रह्मस्वभावो हि प्रपञ्चः, न प्रपञ्चस्वभावं ब्रह्म. 

(Brahma Sutra 3.2.21 darshanAchcha in ubhayali~NgAdhikaraNam)
brahmasvabhAvo hi prapa~nchaH, na prapa~nchasvabhAvaM brahma

For this phenomenal universe of manifestations has Brahman as its essence and not that Brahman has the phenomenal manifestations as Its essence.

Please elaborate this statement.
