A time for all to dwell upon the great Master's teaching. When Tapovan Maharaj was very sick and saw Chinmayananda Ji sobbing, it seems, the great Guru, taunted him saying - is this what I have taught you so long!!
Reconciling and aligning the intellectual understanding with the emotional side, theface that interacts with 'samsaara' holds the key to ultimate liberation. If at all any work is there to complete the equation from 'tatwamasi' to 'aham brahmasmi' is this and this only. If one captures this in the first hearing the job is done. If not keep the track with all the upanishad, prakarana granthas, satsanghs, pilgrimages, discussions and many a Gurus, teachings and may be many a births!!
Regards
Balagopal
