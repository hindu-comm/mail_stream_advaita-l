Shri Chandramouliji wrote:

>The issue under discussion is whether such knowledge ( determinate or
indeterminate ) includes all three components “ I “ ( pramatru ) , “
recognize “ ( pramana ) and “ this is that >Devadatta “ ( prameya ) or not
. In my understanding , it does.

Since both determinate and indeterminate perceptions fall under the general
category of perception, which is a pramANa, yes, it makes sense to identify
pramAtR, pramANa, and prameya.
