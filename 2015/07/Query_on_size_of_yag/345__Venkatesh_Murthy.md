Namaste Sri Animesh Mahodaya

You are correct we have become ignorant of this issue. Some very
orthodox Brahmin families may prepare and wear it according to Sastra.
In South Indian cities many Brahmins are simply purchasing Yajnopavita
in some shops and wearing the same on Upakarma day. They are following
same practice in giving Daana. Nobody is paying attention to
Yajnopavita size. You are lucky if you can find a Brahmin shopkeeper
selling Yajnopavita. Worse situation is some Brahmins may purchase
from Vaishya shopkeepers. We don't know who has prepared that
Yajnopavita. Kshatriyas are very few in number. Majority of
Yajnopavita purchase is by Brahmins and Vaishyas.
