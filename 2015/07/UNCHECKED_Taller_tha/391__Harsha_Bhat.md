> - True - SOme of the vaishnava scholars I interact really dislike Buddha.
>
> However I see that Buddha was interpreted wrongly.
>
> Buddha brought in the culture of Meditation and through meditation being
> free of all vasanas.
> Shankar brought in the spirit of enquiry and questioning to be free of all
> vasanas.
>
> I feel both are complementary.
>
> *On a second note - why Buddhism will not have a appeal with the masses ?*
>
> - Very intellectual - will make the culture dry and meaningless.
> - Sometimes tends to increase the ego.
> - Too much of analysis.
> - No music and dance to reach the supreme .
>
> I have couple of friends whose faith is close to Buddhism.  I feel that
> they have NO RASA or KAVYA in them.
>
> I think it is here Shankara with his focus on Soundarya and Satyam - with
> music pulls us away from the samsara in a joyous manner.
>
> On this guru poornima day - I bow to him for guiding us to the eternal and
> the timeless.
