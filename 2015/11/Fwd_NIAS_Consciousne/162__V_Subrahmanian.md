A highlight of the announcement brochure:

It is the subject for cutting edge interdisciplinary research in the 21st
century with questions concerning life in general and human mind and
cognition in particular.Because of developments in the areas of
neurosciences, neuropsychology, neurophilosophy, relevant areas of physics
and cosmology over the past 20 years, this field has become an active area
of research in various countries. There have been more than a hundred books
published on the related topics in recent years.

Today, consciousness studies have implications in varied planes of human
interactions questioning our fundamental assumptions about origins of life,
nature of subjective experiences, and human wellbeing. Along side is the
pertinent enquiry into the presence and nature of consciousness in
non-human species.
