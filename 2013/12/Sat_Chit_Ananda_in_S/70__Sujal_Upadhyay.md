Namaste,

sat = Truth
chitta = Consciousness
Ananda = bliss, where there is no sorrow, no fear. there is no fear as
there is no duality.

Truth is one, without a second. Hence it is bliss. This truth is 'I am pure
consciousness'.

these three are not attributes and are not to be experienced separately.
They point to one tatva - Brahman.

OM

Sujal Upadhyay
