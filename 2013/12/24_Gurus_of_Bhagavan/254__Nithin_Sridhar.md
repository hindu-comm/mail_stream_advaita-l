> I've been to that area.  In puranas it is known as vastrapatha kShetra and

> historical monuments (such as shilalekhas of Ashoka, Skandagupta,

> sannyasis are found there.  Some are residents others are parivrajakas
> (wanderers) for whom this is a regular stop on a great pradakshina of
> Bharat.  From Mt. Abu (Arbudachala) they proceed through there on the way
> to Dwaraka/Somanath.
