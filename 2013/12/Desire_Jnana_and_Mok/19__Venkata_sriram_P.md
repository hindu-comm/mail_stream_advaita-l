Namaste,

The jnAnai is whom vAsanAs are not destroyed, cannot be called as jnAni.

He has to take the birth depending upon the strength of the vAsanAs.

jaDabharata is one such example who took birth as a deer owing to his 
strong attachment towards the deer.

regs,
sriram
