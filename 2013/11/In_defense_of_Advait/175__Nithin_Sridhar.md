Varna by birth has lost much of its meaning today as people have forsaken
their duties. Today many non-brahmins exhibit Brahmin temperaments and many
Brahmins exhibit other temperaments. Hence, Jati is not in alignment with
Guna, due to this determination of one's Varna Dharma has become
complicated.

- Nithin
