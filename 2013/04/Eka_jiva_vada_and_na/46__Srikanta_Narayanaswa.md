In both the sutras and bhashyas by Sri.Shankara,nowhere it is mentioned Eka jiva vada.It is more implied as Eka-Atma vada rather than Eka jiva vada.From the nana jiva vada one has to move towards the Eka-Atma vada.
N.Srikanta.
