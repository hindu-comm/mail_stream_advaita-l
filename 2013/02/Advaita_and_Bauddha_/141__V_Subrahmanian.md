> I do not understand this. Why Swami Vivekananda is saying Bauddha Dharma
> is coming by Yoganubhuti  from Vedanta Advaita Vada? Kindly explain.

I think Swamiji is saying that the other popular world
religion-philosophies have their basis in the Indian Vedic system.  While
saying that, he states that the 'bauddha' dharma's denying the vyavaharika
prapancha (that there is no dvaita) has its parallel in the Advaita
doctrine.  An aspirant of bauddha system will come to realize this through
yogic experience.

I am reminded of a somewhat similar statement in the Kannada book 'mata
traya sameekShA' authored by the noted Madhwa scholar Dr.A.V.Nagasampige.
In this book, under 'Advaita', the author devotes a section to establish
that Advaita Vedanta is very different from buddhism.  He concludes by
saying: 'since the Bauddhas have formulated their system based on the
nirvishesha Brahman of the Upanishads, they are really 'pracchanna
vaidika-s'.
