Dear Sri Bhaskar
A professional man has to ensure his attire is in consonance with the 
tradition.A purohith has to wear panchakacham and uthariyam during his 
duties of performing pooja etc on behalf of the yajaman.I he wears to 
bermuda I do not think he will earn respect.R.Krishnamoorthy.
