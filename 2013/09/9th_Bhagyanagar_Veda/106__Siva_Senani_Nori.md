Nori Narasimha Sastry Chartiable Trust is pleased to announce that the 9th Bhagyanagar Veda Sabha would be held on the 15th of September 2013, Sunday at Kalyana Mandapam, next to Ramalayam, Nallakunta, Hyderabad. The sabha would start at 8:30 am and end by 7:30 pm.The pareekshaa-paddhati would be followed. A test is being conducted for kramaantasvaadhyaayins on 14th and those who pass that would sit along with the adhyaapakas on the 15th.All those who believe in the praamaaNyataa of the Veda are welcome.
