Very true. Our system is based on certain unquestionable vaidika pramanas
that are more important than people, logic, eloquence, argumentative skills
and intellectual brilliance...on either side of a discussion.

One line of right pramana quoted in favor of a point could convince and
convert more opposing postures than a hundred arguments, reasonings and
substantiations.
