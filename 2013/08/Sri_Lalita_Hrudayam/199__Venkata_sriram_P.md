The problem is not with "d" or "t".

The word "sarvAsampatpradaM" should be "sarva-sampatpradaM".

The deerga a-kAra gives the meaning as "sarva-asampatkaraM"  ie., bestower of asampat (inauspiciousness).  The artha is vipareeta.
