Namaste.

>> All these rituals are for those who think that I am body, I am doing etc?
Yes, for those who think so and also for those who have and adhere
to preferences/norms in other walks of life.
If we adhere to rules set by someone in other walks of life, we should be
ten times more eager to adhere to the rules set in our dharma SAstra-s and
when despite our eagerness it is not possible then confess our inability
and go about with the next best alternative.

dharmasya jayOstu.

-Sarma
