Dear All,

Thanks for immediate response to my doubt.

What message should we take from Shankara's Adhyasa Bhashya? Should we
accept it or reject it? Should we not try to forget that ' I am body'  even
after reading this Bhashya? It is clearly stated by Bhagavatpada that
Adhyas is the root cause for all evils in this world, in his last sentence.
"ASYA ANARTHAHETO ADHYASASYA PRAHANAYA  ATMAIKATVA VIDYA PRATIPATTAYE SARVE
VEDANTA PRAVARTANTE"

 ( I know very much that it is not easy to do it).

Kind regards.

Subramanya UH
