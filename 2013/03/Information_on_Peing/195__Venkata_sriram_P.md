Namaste,

It is paingarAhasya brAhmaNa which is the brAhmaNa bhAga of Rg Veda. 

There are 4 brAhmaNa bhAgAs of Rg Veda:

1) aitrEya 
2) shAnkhyAyana (kauSitaki)
3) paingirAhasya
4) sAtyAyana

Among the 4 above, only aitrEya & kauSitaki are available now. Rest are lost.
