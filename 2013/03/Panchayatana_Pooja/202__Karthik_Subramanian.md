Abhisheka Dravya include:

Sugandha tailam, tandula churna, panchagavyam, phala/phala-vargam, ghee, milk, curds, honey, fruit juice, tender coconut, sandal paste, kalasha tirtham

This is as per the vaideeka krama followed in TN. Could differ from region to region.
