There will be a two-day seminar on the nyAyAmRta - advaita siddhi texts at
the Purna prajna Samshodhana Mandiram, Bangalore on the 20th and 21st March
2013.  There will be two sessions on both days: 9 Am to 12 noon and 3 PM to
5.30 pm. Dr.Mani Dravid SastriNaH will conduct the Advaita siddhi part and
Dr.Haridasa Bhattacharya and Dr. D.Prahlada Rao will expound on the
nyAyAmRta text.  The topic for the discussion is:
pratikarmavyavasthAbhangaH ( the refutation of the Advaitic explanation of
perception ).

Several scholars have been invited to participate in the seminar which will
be conducted in the Sanskrit medium.

Interested persons are welcome to witness the proceedings.
