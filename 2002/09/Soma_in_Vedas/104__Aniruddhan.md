namaste,

>I thought Turiya referred to a state of consciousness beyond but subsuming
>waking, dreaming and deep sleep. Have I got that wrong? (very likely)

Both Sri Jagannath and yourself are correct. Turiya is the same as
Atman/Brahman, and is the Fourth State, beyond waking, dreaming and
deep-sleep states (actually, it is also the common thread running through
these three states). It is also beyond the three gunas (tamas, rajas and
sattva).

namAmi bhagavatpAda Sam.karam lokaSam.karam
