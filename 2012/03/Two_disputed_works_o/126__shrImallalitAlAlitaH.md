There is a book published by dvArakA-pITha titled
"sha~NkarAchArya-sarvasvam". It contains two disputed works :

   1. yatidaNDaishvaryavidhAnam
   2. laghudharmaprakAshikA (shA~NkarasmR^itiH)

First one is a text related to daNDa of saMnyAsin-s and
shrI-chakra-bhAvanA, etc.
Second one is smR^iti-text.
