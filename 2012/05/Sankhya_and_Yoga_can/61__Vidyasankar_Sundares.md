> There is one more question. Normally when we do Japa or Dhyana of

The wide variety of Asanas taught are meant to gain steadiness in practice.
But for japa, dhyAna, vicAra etc, one needs only padmAsana and the eyes
can well be closed!

In any case, the visualization is internal, in the mind's eye, not with the
external eye.

na sandRSe tishThati rUpam asya na cakshushA paSyati kaScanainam |
