>I think we can cite the Guruvayurappan at Guruvayur, Kerala.  The worship
>services are performed by Nambudiris who are not considered Vaishnavas as

It is true that the nambudiris are not vaishnavas. But the nambudiri
brahmins cannot exactly be treated as smartas either. They consider
themselves as Srautas.

>And what about Ayyappan Temple, Sabarimala?  The deity can be considered

>'Vaishnava' though He is said to be Hari-Hara putran.


The ayyappa temple cannot be considered as vaishnava or saiva. This is
notwithstanding the UrdhvapuNDram.
