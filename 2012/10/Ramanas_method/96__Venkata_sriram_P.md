The path of self-enquiry is verily a traditional method. The dakshimurty stotra says:

bAlyAdiSvapi jAgradAdiSu tathA sarvAsva vasthAsvapi. vyAvrittAsvanu varthamAnam ahaM iti antaH sphurantaM sadA....

Bhagavan is insisting us to look into the source of this *antaH sphuraNa* only.
