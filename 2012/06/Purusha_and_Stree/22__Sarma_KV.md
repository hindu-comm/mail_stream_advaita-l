Sir, Sankara Bhagavatpada said "I am not anything but chidaanandarUpa Siva"
of the advaita sthiti.

So, having "both" maleness and femaleness (or only one of them) is still
not advaita sthiti.. 'having anything' is not advaita aananda sthiti.

-Sarma
