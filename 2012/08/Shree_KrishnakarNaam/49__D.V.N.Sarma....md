Dear Sadananda,

Potana the author of Telugu Bhagavatam comes from a family of Saivas. It is
said that he has written two saiva
prabandhas, Udbhataradhya Charitra and Veerabhadra Vijayam prior to
Bhagavatam. He was actually meditating
on Maheswara when Rama appeared before him ordered him to translate
Bhagavata.

-- 
regards,
Sarma.
