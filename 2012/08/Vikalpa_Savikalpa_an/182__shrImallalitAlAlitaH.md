If AtmAkAravR^itti is accepted in samAdhi, then it's sampraGYAta-samAdhi or
savikalpa-samAdhi.
If there is no vR^itti, then it's asaMpraGYAta-samAdhi or
nirvikalpa-samAdhi.
This is my position.

I insist that this applies to pa~nchadashI-system.
Why ?
Because vidyAraNya has explained two samAdhi-s in jIvanmukti-viveka and he
explains the samAdhi-s according to yoga-sUtra-s.
A short writing in sanskrit is here to show that how nirvikalpaka-samAdhi
is same as asaMpraGYAta-samAdhi :
http://eng.lalitaalaalitah.com/2012/08/oneness-of-nirvikalpa-asaMpraGYAta-samAdhi.html
