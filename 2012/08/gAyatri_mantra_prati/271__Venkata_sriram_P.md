Namaste,

Here is a short pratipadArtha & tAtparya of gAyatri mahAmantra
by Brahmasri Mudigonda Venkatarama Sastry. His is also known as
"brahmavidya alankara" and was instrumental in establishing
the historicity of Sringeri Peeta and the date of Sankara. 

He studied under Mahamahopadhyaya Vemuri Ramabrahmayya Sastry 
and was the favourite discple of Chandrasekhara Bharati Swamigal.
He took srividya diksha under Chandrasekhara Bharati Swamigal.
