Book Back Cover-

Author P Sankaranarayanan was a student of Dr. Radhakrishnan at Madras
Presidency College and later served as a Lecturer and Professor in
various colleges; Palghat, Mangalore, Tellicherry, Kumbakonam, the
Madras Presidency College and Government Arts College.

After retirement from Government service he became the Head of the
Department of Philosophy, Vivekananda College, Madras and was a
Professor at the Bhavan's College in Bombay.

He was a devout Asthika and follower of Vaidika Dharma and a great
devotee of His Holiness Jagadguru Sri Chandrasekharendra Saraswati,
the Paramacharya of Kanchi Kamakoti Peetha.

He also rendered into English the Sanskrit Commentary of the late Sri
Chandrasekhara Bharati Mahaswamigal, on Sankara's Viveka Chudamani,
and Sankara's Commentary on Sri Vishnu Sahasranama Stotra, both
published by the Bhavan.
