>In the tradition, people do a puja and read the Prahlada Charitram from the
>Srimad Bhagavatam.

The धर्मसिन्धु also mentions the arghya mantra for Narasimha. The arghya has
to be given at the end of the pUjA that is performed in the evening.

परित्राणाय साधूनां जातो विष्णुर्नृकेसरी ।

गृहाणार्घ्यं मया दत्तं सलक्ष्मीर्नृहरिः स्वयम् ॥

Anand
