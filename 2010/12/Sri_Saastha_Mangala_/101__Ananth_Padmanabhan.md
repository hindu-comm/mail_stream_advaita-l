> I happen to listen to the Mangala Stotra rendered by Dr Thiagarajan, Chennai. I would request the learned list members to provide some insight in to the source of this. I have given below the sloka as I heard and I might have misspelled the words too. I appreciate the correct words and the meaning.I do not have the sanskrit original and I have given below what I heard. 
> 
> vandhE padma bavArchitham hariharAa nandhAthmajam ma~njulam
> vandhE ratna sathkalA bhavapu~sam sadh dharma samrakshakam
> vandhE subhra vibhUthi bhUshanamayam vEtro jwalam chinmayam
> vandhE rAjikarAja vipulam sampathsa medham prabhum
> panchA dhrEshwara mangalam harihara premA kruthE mangalam
> pinchA lankrujA lankrudha mangalam pranamadhAam chinthA vanE mangalam
> panchAsya dwaja mangalam thrijagadhAm Aadhyam prabhO Mangalam
> panchAsyO mama mangalam susirOlankAra sammangalam
> 
> with pranaam
