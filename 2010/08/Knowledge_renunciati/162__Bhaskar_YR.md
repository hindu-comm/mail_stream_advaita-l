can attain the self realization by following the japa, upavAsa, 
devatArAdhana etc. which are not shAstra viruddha. Shankara says in sUtra 
bhAshya (3-4-38) :  vidhurAdee nAma viruddhaiH purushamAtra saMbandhibhiH 
japOpavAsa devatArAdhanAdibhiH dharma visheshaiH anugrahO vidyAyAH 
saMbhavati.
