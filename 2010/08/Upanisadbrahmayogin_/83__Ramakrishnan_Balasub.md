Up. Brahmayogin has commented on all 108 upanishads in the muktika. All
these are available as Adyar Library publications grouped under categories -
shaiva, vaiShNava, shAkta, vedAnta, yoga, and 10 principal upnaishads.
Translations of these are available as separate publications.
