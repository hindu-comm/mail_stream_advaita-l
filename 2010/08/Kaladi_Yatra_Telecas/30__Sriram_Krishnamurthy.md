Telecast of “Kalady Vijaya Yatra”
The glorious Vijaya Yatra of Jagadguru Shankaracharya Sri Bharati Tirtha
Mahaswamiji to Kalady, the Janma Bhoomi Kshetram of Sri Adi Shankaracharya
on the occasion of the centenary of the rediscovery of the hallowed place by
the 33rd Acharya of the Sringeri Sharada Peetham, Jagadguru Sri
Sacchidananda Shivabhinava Narasimha Bharati Mahaswamiji; will be telecast
on Sri Shankara TV between the 2nd and the 20th of August 2010 on all
weekdays from 9:00 to 9:30 PM IST. Those devotees who do not receive the
aforementioned television channel can watch the programme online at the
official website of the channel at http://www.srisankaratv.net/.

Thanks and Regards,
Sriram
