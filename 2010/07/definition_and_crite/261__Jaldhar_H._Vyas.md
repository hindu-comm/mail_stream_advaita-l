In an epistemological sense, Advaitins (following the Bhatta Mimamsakas) 
accept six pramanas or valid means of knowledge.  Shri Sadananda wrote 
extensively on this earlier this year.

The bottom line is that after all falsehoods have been negated, what 
remains -- Brahman, is truth.
