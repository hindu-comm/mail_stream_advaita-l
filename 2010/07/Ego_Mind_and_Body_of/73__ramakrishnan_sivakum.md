> Jivan mukta has sthula sarira made of pancha bhutas and sukshuma sarira made
> of manas, buddhi and ahankara, remains of prarabda karma. The only
> difference mind is devoid of desires and past impressions. Hence it remains
> actionless or acts for the welfare off the world by Isvara sankalpa. If
> there is action, it is very similar to the actions of an avatara of Isvara.
> The only difference iss that avataras generally does not have a body made of
> pancha bhutas but is aprakrta and not a product of karma. In both cases
> (sadhana siddhas and avatara), sat cit ananda svarupan of suddha caitanyam
> is unobstructed as there is no ignorance to hide it.
