Namaste.
Sri.V.Subramanian had Emailed asking the name of the commentary by
Mahamahopadhyaya Abhayankar Vasudeva Sasri on the book"Siddhanta Bindu"by
Sri.Madhusudhana Saraswathi.
The name of the commentary is --Siddhanta Panjara by Vinayaka.
The book is listed in the publications of The Indian book.com in May 1982.
The number of the publication is -12806.
It is translated into English by K.Kunjunni Raja and published by the
Adyar Library and Research Centre.
