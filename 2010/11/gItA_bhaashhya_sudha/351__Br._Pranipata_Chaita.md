Hari Om Shri Ramakrishna Upadrstaji, Pranaams!

In accordance with AcAryaji's bhaja-govindam it is said that bhagavat-gItA 
should be read and ganga-jala should be drunk only in small doses. bindu 
denote svarUpa. Other amrutas can give jarA-maraNa-mokSha but 
Atma-jnAna-amruta gives abhayam.

Thank you for your series - gItA-bAshya-sudhA-bindavaH.

In Shri Guru Smriti,
Br. Pranipata Chaitanya
