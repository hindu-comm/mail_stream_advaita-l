> > This pa~ncaayatanam is named as Siva
> pa~ncaayatanam or Sakthi
> > pa~ncaayatanam or Vishnu pa~ncaayatanam or Aditya
> pa~ncaayatanam or
> > Ganesa pa~ncaayatanam depending upon which

> Vidwat Sadas, convened by
> > Kanchi Sri Mahaswami, at Narayanapuram, Madurai,
> Tamilnadu 1963)
> >
> > In Siva pa~ncaayatanam , Siva is throned at the
> center, Aditya at
> > Southeast, Gananatha at the Southwest, Ambika at
> the Northwest and
> > Vishnu at the Northeast.
> >
> > I am not sure of the positionings in other
> pa~ncaayatana pUjas.
> 
> The posistionings in other pa~ncaayatana pUjas are
> as follows:
> 
> Ganapathy pa~ncaayatana pUja
> 
> Ganapathy at the center, Vishnu at Northeast, Siva
> at Southeast, Adithya
> Southwest and Ambal at Northwest
> 
> Vishnu pa~ncaayatana pUja
> 
> Vishnu at center, Siva at Northeast, Ganapathy at
> Southeast, Adithya at
> Southwest and Ambal at Northwest
> 
> Ambal pa~ncaayatana pUja
> 
> Ambal at center, Vishnu at Northeast, Siva at
> Southeast, Ganapathy at
> Southwest and Adithya at Northwest
> 
> Adithya pa~ncaayatana pUja
> 
> Adithya at center, Siva at Northeast, Ganapathy at
> Southeast, Vishnu at
> Southwest, Ambal at Northwest
>
