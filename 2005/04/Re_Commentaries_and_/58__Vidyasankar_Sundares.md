Just thought I'd mention that rAmabrahmAnanda tIrtha is the same as abhinava
Sankara, the author of the commentary on the rudrapraSna of yajurveda. The
name abhinava Sankara was probably a title, rAmabrahmAnanda tIrtha being the
proper sannyAsa name.
