The collected works of Ramana Maharshi has the last portion titled as
"VichAra maNi mAlA", with the following note:
"This is a compilation of the salient points extracted by Bhagavan Sri
Ramana Maharshi from a large volume in Tamil known as Vichara Sagara
(Ocean of Enquiry), which itself was a translation from the original in
Hindi by Mahatma Nischaldas..."
